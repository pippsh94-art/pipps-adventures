# Play Call Sheet - Game Day Reference

## Formation Calls & Personnel

### **Personnel Packages**
| **Package** | **Players** | **When to Use** |
|------------|------------|-----------------|
| **11** | 1 RB, 1 TE, 3 WR | Base offense, balanced |
| **10** | 1 RB, 0 TE, 4 WR | Passing downs, spread |
| **12** | 1 RB, 2 TE, 2 WR | Power running, short yardage |
| **21** | 2 RB, 1 TE, 2 WR | Heavy running, goal line |

### **Formation Terminology**
- **"Bunch Right":** 3x1 formation, bunch to the right
- **"Bunch Left":** 3x1 formation, bunch to the left
- **"Trips Right":** 3x1 spread formation right
- **"Trips Left":** 3x1 spread formation left
- **"Tight":** Bunch with 1-yard spacing
- **"Wide":** Bunch with 3-yard spacing

## Down & Distance Tendencies

### **1st Down**
- **1st & 10:** 60% run, 40% pass
- **Calls:** IZ, OZ, Play action, Stick
- **Goal:** Stay ahead of chains

### **2nd Down**  
- **2nd & 5-7:** 50% run, 50% pass
- **2nd & 8+:** 30% run, 70% pass
- **Calls:** Based on previous play success

### **3rd Down**
- **3rd & 1-3:** 80% run, 20% pass
- **3rd & 4-7:** 20% run, 80% pass  
- **3rd & 8+:** 10% run, 90% pass
- **Goal:** Convert at all costs

### **4th Down**
- **Own territory:** Punt
- **Midfield:** Consider situation
- **Opponent territory:** Go for it
- **Goal line:** Almost always go

## Weather Adjustments

### **Windy Conditions**
- **Avoid:** Deep passes into wind
- **Use:** Short passing, power runs
- **Direction:** Throw with wind when possible

### **Rain/Wet Field**
- **Avoid:** Outside runs, timing routes
- **Use:** Inside runs, quick slants
- **Footing:** Expect slips on cuts

### **Cold Weather**
- **Avoid:** Timing passes (ball hard)
- **Use:** Power running game
- **Hands:** Expect drops early

## Coaching Communication

### **Sideline to Field**
- **Hand Signals:** Formation calls
- **Wrist Coach:** Play concepts numbered
- **Verbal:** Only if necessary (crowd noise)

### **Quarterback to Team**
- **Huddle:** Clear, confident delivery
- **Line:** Protection and hot route alerts
- **Cadence:** Consistent rhythm and hard count
