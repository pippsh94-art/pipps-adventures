Code of the Northmen

Northmen Guild's Code of Ethics — A creed for craftsmen, warriors, and those who live with intention.

🔨 Craft & Calling
⚒️Love what you do
❤️Do what you love
📍Begin where you are
🛠️Use what you have
💪Do what you can
🔧Keep your craft and your skills strong
✋Hands, not machines
🏺Craft, not business
⭐Quality, not quantity
💎Value, not price
🎨To create, not to produce
🔍Focus on the detail
🖊️Always leave your mark
🛡️ Tribe & Loyalty
🧓Honor the elders
👦Teach the young
🏠Protect and love your family
🤝Be loyal to your friends
🗣️Voice your opinion
🦶Stand your ground
🔒Trust your brothers
👑Take charge when others show weakness
⚔️ Character
💼Work hard, stay humble
🦁Be brave
🌊Be calm
⏳Be patient
🌅Wake up early
💪Temper your body
🧠 Self-Mastery
📚Learn from your own mistakes, not only from others
👁️Look for the log in your own eye — not the splinter in your brother's
💰Have more, spend less
⏰More time, less convenience
🌲 Nature & Rhythm
🌿Live in nature
🌲Walk in the woods
⛰️Climb the mountains
🧘Find time to be alone
🦌Hunt when you need food
😴Rest when you can
⚒️Work when you must
"Begin where you are. Use what you have. Do what you can." — The Northmen Code, distilled.
