# Game Day Quick Reference Card

## Zone Blocking - Key Calls

| **Inside Zone** | **Outside Zone** | **Power O** |
|----------------|-----------------|-------------|
| "52 is the Mike" | "Reach! Reach!" | "Power O, Power O" |
| Work big-on-big | Get width first | Pull and kick |
| Climb to LBs | Cut on overrun | FB leads |

---

## Bunch Passing - Core 4

### 1. **Stick (vs Zone)**
**Call:** "Stick Right"  
**Read:** Middle LB  
**Hot:** Slant if blitzed

### 2. **Smash (vs Man)** 
**Call:** "Smash Right"  
**Read:** Corner/Safety  
**Hot:** Speed out underneath

### 3. **Mills (Flood)**
**Call:** "Mills Right"  
**Read:** Inside-out  
**Hot:** Drag checkdown

### 4. **Four Verts**
**Call:** "Verticals"  
**Read:** Coverage  
**Hot:** Convert by leverage

---

## RPO Package

| **Zone + Slant** | **Zone + Bubble** | **Zone + Stick** |
|-----------------|------------------|-----------------|
| Read: ILB | Count box (6+) | Read: MLB leverage |
| Steps up = Throw | Throw if outnumbered | Over top = Hand off |
| Sits = Hand off | Even = Run | Under = Throw |

---

## Situational Calls

### **3rd & Short (1-3 yards)**
1. Power O (1st choice)
2. Inside Zone (2nd choice)  
3. QB Sneak (goal line)

### **3rd & Medium (4-7 yards)**
1. Stick Concept
2. Smash Concept
3. Quick slants

### **3rd & Long (8+ yards)**
1. Four Verticals
2. Mills Concept  
3. Deep comeback

### **Red Zone (Inside 20)**
1. Fade combinations
2. Pick plays (bunch)
3. Power O/Inside Zone

### **Two-Minute Drill**
1. Quick slants
2. Hitches to sideline
3. Comeback routes
4. Bubble screens

---

## Coverage Recognition

| **Pre-Snap Read** | **Coverage** | **Best Concept** |
|------------------|-------------|-----------------|
| 1 High Safety | Cover 3/1 | Four Verts, Mills |
| 2 High Safeties | Cover 2/4 | Stick, Smash |
| Safeties Rolling | Cover 2 Roll | Verticals away |
| LBs Crowding | Man/Blitz | Quick slants |

---

## Audible System

### **At the Line**
- **"Kill! Kill!"** = Change play
- **"Alert! Alert!"** = Hot route
- **"Check! Check!"** = RPO read

### **Protection Changes**
- **"55 is the Mike!"** = ID new Mike LB
- **"Razor! Razor!"** = RB protection right
- **"Laser! Laser!"** = RB protection left

---

## Clock Management

### **Hurry-Up Tempo**
- No huddle, signal plays
- Quick snaps on QB count
- Sideline routes to stop clock

### **Eating Clock**
- Inside Zone (safe)
- Stay in bounds
- Use full play clock

---

## Weather Adjustments

### **Wind/Rain**
- Shorter passing game
- More inside zone
- Avoid deep shots

### **Muddy Field**  
- Power O over zone
- Quick slants over timing
- Screen game limited
