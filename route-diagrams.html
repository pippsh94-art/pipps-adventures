  • Open Hand: Out route (3)

  • Circular Motion: Comeback (4/6)
  • Diagonal Point: Corner (7)

Coaching Clinic Notes

Teaching Route Running

  1. Alignment: Consistent splits and stances
  2. Release: Beat press coverage techniques
  3. Stem: Sell the route, set up breaks
  4. Break: Sharp cuts, plant and drive
  5. Finish: Catch technique and YAC

Timing Development

  • 3-Step Drops: Quick game (0, 1, 4 routes)
  • 5-Step Drops: Intermediate (2, 3, 6 routes)
  • 7-Step Drops: Deep shots (7, 8, 9 routes)

Coverage Recognition

  • High Safety Count: 1 or 2 high safeties
  • Linebacker Leverage: Over/under routes
  • Corner Technique: Press/off coverage
  • Safety Rotation: Pre/post snap movement
