Bunch Passing — Core 4

1. Stick (vs Zone)

Call: "Stick Right"
Read: Middle LB
Hot: Slant if blitzed

2. Smash (vs Man)

Call: "Smash Right"
Read: Corner/Safety
Hot: Speed out underneath

3. Mills (Flood)

Call: "Mills Right"
Read: Inside-out
Hot: Drag checkdown

4. Four Verts

Call: "Verticals"
Read: Coverage
Hot: Convert by leverage

  1. Stick Concept

3rd & Long (8+ yards)

  2. Mills Concept
  3. Deep comeback

Audible System

At the Line

  • "Kill! Kill!" = Change play
  • "Alert! Alert!" = Hot route
  • "Check! Check!" = RPO read

Protection Changes

  • "55 is the Mike!" = ID new Mike LB
  • "Razor! Razor!" = RB protection right
  • "Laser! Laser!" = RB protection left

Clock Management

Hurry-Up Tempo

  • No huddle, signal plays
  • Quick snaps on QB count
  • Sideline routes to stop clock

Eating Clock

  • Inside Zone (safe)
  • Stay in bounds
  • Use full play clock

Weather Adjustments

Wind/Rain

  • Shorter passing game
  • More inside zone
  • Avoid deep shots

Muddy Field

  • Power O over zone
  • Quick slants over timing
  • Screen game limited
