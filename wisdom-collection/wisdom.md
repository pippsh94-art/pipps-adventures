## The Man Who Stands Alone
*Added: April 9, 2026*

1. He makes big calls alone and lives with them alone. Quitting something. Walking away. Taking risks. No one to run it past. No one saying yeah back yourself. Just him deciding… and wearing whatever comes after.

2. When things go bad, he doesn't look for anyone. Bad news hits and there's no instinct to call. No "who do I talk to?" Just that familiar feeling… alright, deal with it.

3. He learned most things the hard way. Trusting the wrong people. Getting burned. Backing the wrong move. No one pulled him aside and gave him game. Life taught him by making him feel it.

4. He carries pressure like it's normal. Bills. Stress. Shit not going to plan. You won't hear much about it. He just absorbs it and keeps moving.

5. Being supported feels unfamiliar as hell. Someone backs him, checks in, stands beside him. He appreciates it… but part of him doesn't know what to do with it.

6. It always feels like it's on him. Outcomes. Direction. Getting it right. No sense of a safety net. Just that quiet pressure… if I don't handle it, no one will. He didn't grow up with someone in his corner. So he became the one who stands alone.
