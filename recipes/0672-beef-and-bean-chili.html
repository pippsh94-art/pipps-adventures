Beef and bean chili

Original cookbook page 672

Ingredients

  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1/4 tsp table salt
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes, or more to taste
  • 28 oz canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup canned beef broth, reduced-sodium
  • 8 oz canned tomato sauce
  • 30 oz canned kidney beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup shallot, chopped

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a large pot over medium-high heat. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add shallot and cook until softened.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and beef broth. Stir to combine.
  4. Add bay leaves, salt, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Add kidney beans, rinsed and drained.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Remove bay leaves before serving.
