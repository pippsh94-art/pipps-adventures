Zucchini Tortillas

Original cookbook page 288

Ingredients

  • * 2 medium zucchini, shredded
  • *1 cup mozzarella, shredded
  • * 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • * 1/4 cup coconut flour (or almond flour) *
  • *1tsp garlic powder
  • *1 tsp dried oregano
  • * salt to taste
  • * olive oil spray

Instructions

  1. * Preheat your oven to 350°F.
  2. Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. * After coarsley grating your zucchini, place half the shreds in a cheese cloth (or thin kitchen towel) and squeeze out as much of the liquids as you can.
  4. * To a large bowl, add the zucchini, mozzarella, eggs, flour, oregano, garlic powder and salt.
  5. Mix until completely incorporated.
  6. If there is excess liquid after mixing, sprinkle in 1 more tablespoon of flour to absorb — mine didn't need it.
  7. Form large balls and then flatten them out with your fingertips on the baking sheet, keeping the round tortilla shap.
  8. Spray the tops with olive oil and bake for about 25 minutes or until aolden on top.
