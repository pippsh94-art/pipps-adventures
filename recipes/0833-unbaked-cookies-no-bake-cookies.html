Unbaked Cookies (No-Bake Cookies)

Original handwritten recipe card

Classic no-bake chocolate peanut butter oatmeal cookies from a handwritten recipe card.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 3 cups quick cooking oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter

Instructions

  1. Combine sugar, cocoa, butter, and milk in a saucepan. Bring to a boil for 1 minute.
  2. Stir in 3 cups quick cooking oatmeal and 1/2 cup peanut butter.
  3. Make drop cookies on greased sheet or on a sheet of wax paper (handi-wrap).
  4. Let cool until set.
