Hamburger Potato Casserole

Original cookbook page 700

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4-5 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1small onion, chopped
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1.can cream of mushroom soup (10.5 oz)
  • 4/2 cup milk
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: garlic powder or other seasonings

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Brown the ground beef with chopped onions in a skillet, : salt and pepper. Layer the sliced potatoes in a greased casserole dish. Spread the cooked beef and onions over the potatoes. In a bowl, mix the cream of mushroom soup with milk, an mixture over the casserole. Top with shredded cheddar cheese.
