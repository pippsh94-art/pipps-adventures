Skillet Chili Mac

Original cookbook page 61

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces whole-wheat elbow noodles, rotini or fusilli
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it apart as it cooks
  2. Drain excess fat if needed
  3. Add diced onions and bell peppers, cook until softened
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and chili powder
  5. Add cooked pasta and mix well
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  7. Simmer for 10-15 minutes to allow flavors to blend
  8. Serve hot, topped with shredded cheese if desired
