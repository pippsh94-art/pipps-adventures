Cottage Cheese Brownie

Original cookbook page 832

Ingredients

  • 1egg
  • 1/3 cup cottage cheese
  • 3 tbsp cocoa
  • 3 tbsp monk fruit
  • chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Lightly grease a small ramekin (or dish) with oil. Pour the brownie mixture into the dish and top with chocolate chips. . Microwave 1.5-2min until desired firmness. Enjoy!
