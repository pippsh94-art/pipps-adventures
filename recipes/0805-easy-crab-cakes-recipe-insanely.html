Easy Crab Cakes Recipe - Insanely

Original cookbook page 805

Ingredients

  • 1 pound jumbo lump crab meat, picked through 1/2 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon fresh parsley (chopped) 1egg
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground dry mustard 1 teaspoon Old Bay Seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • zest of 1 lemon 1/4 cup vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  3. Form into 8 patties, place them on a baking tray, and cover with plastic wrap. Refrigerate f hour to allow the cakes to firm up. (This will keep them from cracking/breaking in the pan).
  4. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. Cook the crab cakes in batches for 3-5 minutes per side or until golden brown.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy! Notes + You may need to add more breadcrumbs, depending on how moist the crab meat is. Start add more, as needed.
