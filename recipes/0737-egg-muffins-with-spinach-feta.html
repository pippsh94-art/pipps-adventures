Egg Muffins With Spinach & Feta

Original cookbook page 737

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk (or dairy-free alternative)
  • 1 cup fresh spinach, chopped
  • 1/3 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, diced (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C / 375°F. Lightly grease 6 cup muffin tin with cooking spray. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt, pepper, and ore combined. Divide spinach and feta evenly among muffin cups. Ac tomatoes if desired. Pour egg mixture evenly into each muffin cup, filling tk full. Bake for 20-25 mins, or until the egg muffins are set a golden on top. Allow muffins to cool for 5 mins before removing them Serve warm or store in the fridge for a quick breakfast Tips: These muffins can be stored in an airtight container in up to 4 days.
