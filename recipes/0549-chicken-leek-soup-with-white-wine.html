Chicken Leek Soup with White Wine

Original cookbook page 549

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 pounds chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1large leek, cut into thin rounds
  • Agreen onions (scallions), chopped
  • Acelery sticks, chopped
  • 1small cabbage, cut into thick slices
  • 1cup white wine
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • pinch of nutmeg
  • 3 cups water

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken breast pieces and cook until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pot, add the leek, green onions, and celery. Saute until softened.
  4. Add the cabbage and cook for a few minutes.
  5. Pour in the white wine and let it reduce slightly.
  6. Add the water, salt and pepper, paprika, and nutmeg.
  7. Return the chicken to the pot and bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce heat and simmer until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender, about 30 minutes.
