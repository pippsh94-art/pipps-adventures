Antipasto Smothered Chicken

Original cookbook page 56

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • 1/2 cup chopped marinated artichoke hearts
  • 1/4 cup chopped salami
  • 1/4 cup roasted red peppers, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons red-wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano, plus more for garnish
  • 1 pound chicken cutlets
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 2 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat broiler to high.
  2. Mix 2 tablespoons oil, artichoke hearts, salami, peppers, and 1 tablespoon vinegar in a medium bowl. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the remaining 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Season the chicken with salt and pepper and add to the skillet, stirring to brown, about 3 minutes. Turn the chicken over and top with the artichoke mixture and cheese. Transfer the pan to the oven and broil the chicken until the cheese is browned and an instant-read thermometer inserted in the thickest part of the registers 165°F, 3 to 4 minutes more. Serve the chicken with any pan drippings and oregano, if desired.
