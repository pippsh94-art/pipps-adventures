Cheesy Beef Enchilada Casserole

Original cookbook page 69

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons avocado or canola oil
  • 8 ounces 90%-lean ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 medium red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 cup frozen corn kernels
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 1/2 cups Cheese Sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add garlic, peppers, onion and spices, cook until softened.
  5. See recipe image for full details.
