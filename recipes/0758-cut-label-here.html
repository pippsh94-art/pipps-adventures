Ingredients

  • 1 Ib boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/4 pound carrots, peeled and diced (1 cup) (about 2 carrots)
  • 1 cup frozen corn (half of a 10 oz bag)
  • 1 can (1502 per can) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can (280z per can) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon adobo sauce from a 7oz can of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 32 oz chicken broth (not needed until day of cooking), 4 cups
  • 1 cup crushed tortilla chips (not needed until day of cooking)
  • To Cook in Crockpot
  • Add to crockpot with broth and tortilla chips.
  • To Cook in Instant Pot
  • Pressure cook 25 minutes.
  • Natural release.
  • Shred chicken.
  • Stir in crushed tortilla chips and serve.
  • USE BY:

Instructions

  1. Add to crockpot with broth and tortilla chips. Cook on "low" setting for 6-8 hours. Shred chicken and stir. Add to instant pot with broth. Stir in crushed tortilla chips and serve.
