Rhubarb Crisp

Original handwritten recipe card (front and back)

Classic rhubarb crisp with an oat crumble topping from a handwritten recipe card.

Bake: 35 minutes • Temp: 375°F

Ingredients

  • 5 cups rhubarb, cut up
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Mix cut rhubarb with 1 cup sugar, 3 tablespoons flour, and cinnamon. Spread in a baking dish.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine oats, brown sugar, 1/2 cup flour, and melted butter to make the crumble topping.
  4. Spread the topping evenly over the rhubarb mixture.
  5. Bake at 375°F for about 35 minutes until golden and bubbly.
