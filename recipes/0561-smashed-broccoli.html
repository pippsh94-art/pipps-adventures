Instructions

  1. 1 broccoli in florets cooked in salted water
  2. 1 Cook broccoli so that it is not too mushy but can be easily mashed on a baking sheet.
  3. 2 Place florets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and mash with a glass.
  4. 3 Coat with a mixture of olive oil, salt and paprika noble sweet.
  5. 4 Sprinkle grated parmesan over the top and bake in the oven at 220 degrees until the broccoli is nice and crispy around the edges.
