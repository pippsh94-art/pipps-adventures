Chuck Roast For Instant Pot

Original cookbook page 238

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs beef
  • chuck, roast
  • see notes for

Instructions

  1. Juicy and tender instant pot pot roast and potatoes with gravy makes the perfect
  2. READY IN: Ihr 15mins = SERVES: 4
  3. chuck, roast see notes for instructions from frozen
  4. Turn on your instant pot and set it to "saute".
  5. Ina small bowl stir together salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and smoked paprika.
  6. Rub mixture all over the roast to coat all sides.
  7. Drizzle oil in instant pot, wait about 30 seconds, then use tongs to place roast in the pot.
  8. Use tongs to turn the roast onto another side for 3-4 minutes, repeating until all sides are browned.
  9. Switch instant pot to "pressure cook" on high and set to 60-80 minutes (60 for a 3 pound roast, 80 for a 5 pound roast.
  10. Add potatoes, onions, and carrots to pot (just arrange them around the roast) and pour beef broth and worcestershire sauce over everything.
  11. Place lid on the pot and turn to locked position.
  12. When the cooking time is up, do a natural release for 10 minutes (don't touch anything on the pot, just let it de-pressurize on it's own for 10 minutes).
