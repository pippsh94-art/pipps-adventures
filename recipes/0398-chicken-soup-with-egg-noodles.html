Chicken Soup With Egg Noodles

Original cookbook page 398

Ingredients

  • 1(31/2) pound chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 4 (16 ounce) cans low-sodium chicken broth
  • 2 carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced
  • Ya cup chopped onion
  • 1 (8 ounce) package dried egg noodles
  • Y2 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prep Time: 30 mins Cook Time: 45 mins Total Time: 1 hrs 15 mins
  2. 1(31/2) pound chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  3. 2 carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
  4. Y2 cup finely chopped fresh parsley
  5. Combine chicken and chicken broth in a large, heavy pot over medium-high heat; bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat, cover partially, and simmer until chicken is no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 20 minutes.
  7. Remove chicken with tongs and transfer to a large bowl.
  8. Allow chicken and broth to cool slightly.
  9. Remove skin and bones from cooled chicken and discard.
  10. Cut chicken meat into bite-sized pieces.
  11. Skim fat off the top of the cooled broth and discard.
  12. Return chicken broth to a simmer.
  13. Add carrots, celery, and onion to the broth and simmer until vegetables soften, about 8 minutes.
  14. Stir in chicken, egg noodles, parsley, and lemon juice and simmer until noodles are tender, about 5 minutes.
  15. Season with salt and pepper.
