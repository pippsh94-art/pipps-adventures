Ree Drummond Corn Cakes w/ Avo

Original cookbook page 317

Ingredients

  • Avocado Salsa:
  • + 1/3 cup finely diced red onion
  • * 1 avocado, finely diced
  • + lime, zested and juiced
  • + 4 Roma tomato, finely diced
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Corn Cakes:
  • + 1/4 cup crumbled Cotija
  • + 4/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • + teaspoon ground cumin
  • + teaspoon ground paprika
  • + 2 large eggs, beaten
  • + A. cup fresh sweet corn kernels (from about 2
  • ears of corn)
  • + 4-cloves garlic, grated
  • + 2 scallions, thinly sliced
  • + 'lime, zested and juiced
  • + Vegetable oil, for frying
  • + Sea salt, for finishing

Instructions

  1. For the avocado salsa: Mix the red onion, avocado, lime zest and juice and tomato in a small bowl. Season with salt and pepper; set aside.
  2. For the corn cakes: Whisk together the Cotija, flour, cumin, paprika and egg in a medium bow! until incorporated. Add the corn, garlic, scallions and lime zest. Fold together until incorporated. Set aside.
  3. Heat 1/2 inch of oil in a large, heavy-bottomed skillet over medium heat to around 350 degrees F. In batches, add 2-tablespoon scoops of the batter and fry until golden and crisp, 2 to 2 1/2 minutes per side. Remove to a paper towellined plate, sprinkle with sea salt and drizzle over some of the lime juice. Serve the corn cakes hot, topped with the avocado salsa.
