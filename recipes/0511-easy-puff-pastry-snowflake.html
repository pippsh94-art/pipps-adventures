Easy Puff Pastry Snowflake

Original cookbook page 511

Ingredients

  • 1 (17.3-0Z.) package frozen puff pastry
  • 1/3 cup Nutella or jam
  • 1 large egg beaten with 1 Tablespoon

Instructions

  1. Thaw puff pastry sheets and preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Dust work surface with flour. Roll out one sheet of puff pastry.
  3. Spread Nutella or jam evenly over one sheet.
  4. Place the second sheet on top.
  5. Cut into a snowflake pattern using a round guide and knife.
  6. Twist each section of the snowflake shape.
  7. Brush with egg wash and sprinkle with crystal sanding sugar.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden and puffed.
  9. Dust with confectioners sugar before serving.
