Grilled Rosemary Lamb Meatballs

Original cookbook page 154

Ingredients

  • Grilled Rosemary Lamb.
  • 1 lb. ground lamb
  • 1/4 c. seasoned bread crumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp. finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 8 oz. penne, cooked
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. @ Combine ground lamb, bread crumbs, garlic, rosemary, and 1/ teaspoon each salt and pepper. Divide mixture into 16 mound: shape into balls. @) Grill, covered, on medium for 10 minutes, turning occasionalh tomatoes with penne and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Serve meatballs o penne, garnished with parsley. 53g; Total Fat 17g; Saturated Fat 7g; Dietary Fiber 4g; Sodium 540mg
