Grilled Rosemary Lamb Meatballs

Original cookbook page 154

Grilled lamb meatballs seasoned with rosemary and garlic, served over penne pasta with fresh chopped tomatoes.

Cook: 10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground lamb
  • 1/4 c. seasoned bread crumbs
  • 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp. finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 medium tomatoes, chopped
  • 8 oz. penne, cooked
  • Chopped parsley, for garnish

Instructions

  1. Combine ground lamb, bread crumbs, garlic, rosemary, and 1/2 teaspoon each salt and pepper. Divide mixture into 16 mounds; gently shape into balls.
  2. Grill, covered, on medium for 10 minutes, turning occasionally. Toss tomatoes with penne and 1/4 teaspoon salt. Serve meatballs over penne, garnished with parsley.
