Chicken Tagine with Apricots & Olives

Original cookbook page 54

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat
  2. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper, then brown them in the oil
  3. Remove chicken and set aside
  4. In the same pot, sauté onions and garlic until fragrant
  5. Add tomato paste and spices, stirring to combine
  6. Return chicken to the pot, add apricots and olives
  7. Pour in broth and bring to a simmer
  8. Cover and bake in preheated 325 degree oven for 1 hour
  9. Serve over couscous or rice
