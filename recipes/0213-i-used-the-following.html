Ingredients

  • 2 large zucchini
  • Some of the middle of the zucchini, but added 1/2 a diced zucchini
  • 1 lb hamburger
  • 4 mushrooms chopped up
  • 1 can sliced olives chopped up
  • 1 jar Rao's Tomato Basil pasta sauce

Instructions

  1. - some of the middle of the zucchini, but added 1/2c diced zucchini - 1lb hamburger
  2. - 1 can sliced olives chopped up
  3. ( cooked the hamburger.
  4. While it was cooking I cut the zucchini in 1/2 and removed the seeds/ soft center.
  5. Once the hamburger was cooked, I drained off the excess fat and added the onion and garlic and cooked on low until until soft.
  6. Then I added the pasta sauce and the zucchini dices, diced zucchini scraped out of the center (no seeds), mushrooms, olives and Parmesan.
