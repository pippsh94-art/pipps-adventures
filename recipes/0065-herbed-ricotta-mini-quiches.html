Herbed Ricotta Mini Quiches

Original cookbook page 65

Ingredients

  • 12 slices soft whole-wheat bread
  • 4 large eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Mix ricotta cheese with herbs, salt, and pepper
  3. Fill pastry cups or small ramekins with ricotta mixture
  4. Top with grated cheese and bake for 15-20 minutes until set
  5. Cool slightly before serving
