Cauliflower Frittata

Original cookbook page 296

Ingredients

  • 8 strips cooked bacon crumbled
  • 1 cup of shredded cauliflower (riced cauliflower)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 3 pieces fresh basil leaves chopped
  • 3 pcs cherry tomatoes, sliced
  • 3/4 cup cheddar cheese shredded
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. Heat a frying pan and pour-in olive oil Add the cauliflower and fry until cooked Combine eggs, salt, bacon, and heavy cream Whisk and pour ov cauliflower.Cook in low to medium heat for 4 to 5 minutes. Arrange cheese, tomatoes, basil over the egg mixture. Cover the and continue cooking in low heat for 4 to 6 minutes.
