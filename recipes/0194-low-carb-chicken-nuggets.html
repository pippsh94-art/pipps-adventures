Low Carb Chicken Nuggets

Original cookbook page 194

Simple low carb/keto-friendly chicken nuggets made with canned chicken, egg, and cheese, formed into patties and baked until crispy.

Cook: 20 minutes • Total: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 can (10 oz) of chicken
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup of shredded cheese
  • Salt
  • Optional: Kickin' Chicken seasoning and pepper

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Form into small patties.
  3. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  4. Bake at 450°F for 20 minutes, flipping halfway through.
