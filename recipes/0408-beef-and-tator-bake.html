Beef And Tator Bake

Original cookbook page 408

Ingredients

  • 4 cups frozen Tater Tots
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • can (10-3/4 ounces) condensed
  • cream of broccoli soup, undiluted
  • V3 cup 2% milk
  • 1 package (16 ounces) frozen
  • chopped broccoli, thawed
  • 1can (2.8 ounces) french-fried
  • onions, divided
  • 1cup shredded Colby-Monterey Jack
  • cheese, divided
  • 1 medium tomato, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°. Spread Tater Tots evenly in an ungreased 13x9 baking dish. Bake, uncovered, 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, cook over medium heat until no longe pink, 5-7 minutes; crumble meat; drain. Stir in seasonings, soup, milk, broccoli, 3/4 cup onions, 1/2 cup cheese and tomato; heat through. Po over potatoes.
  3. Bake, covered, 20 minutes. Sprinkle with the remaining onions and cheese. Bake, uncovered, until cheese is melted, 5-10 minutes.
