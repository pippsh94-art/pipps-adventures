Grilled Turmeric Chicken -

Original cookbook page 551

Ingredients

  • 8 boneless skinless chicken thighs (about 3 Ibs)
  • 2lemons, juiced
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/4 tsp cumin
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • ° 4cloves fresh garlic, chopped
  • 1/2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tbsp dried dill
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine chicken and rest of ingredients in a large bowl. Mix with hands well. Cover and let marinate for at least 30 minutes but up to overnight.
  2. Preheat grill to medium-high heat. Grill chicken thighs for about 6-7 minutes per side, until internal temperature reaches 165°F.
  3. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
