Instructions

  1. 1 small Spanish onion, halved and thinly sliced
  2. 1 1/2 pounds small new potatoes, par-cooked and diced
  3. 8 ounces Italian sausage, hot, mild or a combination, removed from the casing
  4. 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh rosemary
  5. 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
  6. Heat the butter on a cast-iron griddle or in large saute pan over medium-high.
  7. Add the garlic and onions and cook until softened, 2 to 3 minutes.
  8. Add the peppers and cook another 2 minutes.
  9. Add the potatoes, sausage, paprika and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
  10. Cook, stirring, about 2 minutes.
  11. Stir in the rosemary, and then start pressing the mixture onto the griddle so that the bottom gets brown and crispy and the potatoes stick together.
  12. Turn the mixture occasionally to distribute the crispy bits.
  13. Toss the mixture with the parsley and transfer to a serving platter.
  14. Drizzle with olive oil and serve hot or at room temperature.
