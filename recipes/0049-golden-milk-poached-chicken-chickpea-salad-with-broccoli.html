Golden Milk Poached Chicken & Chickpea Salad with Broccoli

Original cookbook page 49

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon ground cumin
  • 1 tablespoon ground turmeric

Instructions

  1. Whisk milk with spices and bring to a simmer.
  2. Poach chicken in turmeric-infused milk.
  3. See recipe image for full details.
