Potato Artichoke Gratin

Original cookbook page 37

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium leek, white and light green parts only, diagonally sliced 1/2 inch thick
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup reduced-fat milk, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 (14 oz) can artichoke hearts, drained and quartered
  • 2 pounds russet potatoes, peeled and sliced 1/8 inch thick
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F. Coat a 9-by-13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
  2. Heat oil in a medium skillet over high heat. Add leek and cook, stirring, until wilted and starting to brown, about 3 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Combine cream, all but 2 tablespoons milk, salt and pepper in a medium saucepan. Heat over medium until steaming. Whisk flour into the reserved 2 tablespoons milk and stir into the cream mixture. Cook, whisking, until smooth. Remove from heat.
  4. Layer half the potatoes in the prepared dish. Top with the leek and artichoke hearts. Layer remaining potatoes on top.
  5. Pour the cream sauce evenly over the potatoes. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake until the top is golden and potatoes are tender, about 1 hour 30 minutes.
