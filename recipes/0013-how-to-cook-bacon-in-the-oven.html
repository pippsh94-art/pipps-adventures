How To Cook Bacon In The Oven

Original cookbook page 13

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces bacon
  • Gosuare vo.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400. Cover baking sheet with parchment paper. Place bacon on parchment paper. Remove from oven and place on paper towel for 1 minute to cool.
