Oven Baked Chicken Thighs

Original cookbook page 325

Ingredients

  • So, besides the chicken thighs, all you need for this recipe is a f
  • 6 Chicken Thighs, skin and bone on
  • 1 tsp. Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tsp. Garlic Powder
  • 1 tsp. Dried Thyme
  • 1 tbsp. Brown Sugar
  • 2 tbsp. Olive Oil
  • 1/2 tsp. Black Pepper, grounded

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels. Season with smoked paprika, garlic powder, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Place chicken thighs skin-side up on a baking sheet or in a cast iron skillet.
  4. Bake for 35-45 minutes until internal temperature reaches 165°F and skin is golden and crispy.
  5. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
