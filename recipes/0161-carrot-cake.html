Carrot Cake

Original cookbook page 161

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 cups sugar

Instructions

  1. Add legs at a time, then add carrots.
  2. Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
  3. Cool. Then frost with cream cheese frosting.
