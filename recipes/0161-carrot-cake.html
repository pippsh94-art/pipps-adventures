Carrot Cake

Original cookbook page 161

A classic carrot cake recipe passed down from Mom

Cook: 45 min - 1 hr

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 2 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 cups sugar
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1½ cups oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 3 cups grated carrots

Instructions

  1. Grease and flour 9x13 pan or 2-9" cake pans
  2. Mix dry ingredients with oil
  3. Add 1 egg at a time, then add carrots
  4. Bake @ 350° for 45 min - 1 hr
  5. Cool. Then frost with cream cheese frosting
