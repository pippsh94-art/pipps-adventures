Oven Roasted Chicken

Original cookbook page 84

Ingredients

  • Origina
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, giblets removed
  • a) 1 teaspoon salt and black Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Remove giblets from chicken cavity and pat skin dry
  3. Season inside and out with salt, pepper, and onion powder
  4. Rub margarine over the skin
  5. Place in a roasting pan with celery pieces in the cavity
  6. Roast uncovered for 1 hour 15 minutes until internal temperature reaches 180 degrees F
  7. Remove from oven and baste with pan drippings
  8. Cover with foil and let rest 30 minutes before carving
