Keto Pesto Chicken Casserole

Original cookbook page 438

Ingredients

  • 1% lbs boneless chicken thighs
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter or coconut oil
  • %, cup (2% oz.) red pesto or
  • green pesto
  • 1% cups heavy whipping
  • cream
  • 3 oz. (7% cup) pitted olives
  • 5 oz. (1 cup) feta cheese, diced
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • To serve
  • 5 oz. (2% cups) leafy greens

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add butter or oil to a large skillet and fry the chicken pieces in
  4. batches over medium-high heat until golden brown.
  5. Combine pesto and heavy cream in a bowl.
  6. Place the fried chicken pieces in a baking dish together with olives,
  7. feta cheese and garlic. Add the pesto/cream mixture.
  8. Bake in oven for 20-30 minutes, until the dish turns bubbly and light
  9. brown around the edges.
