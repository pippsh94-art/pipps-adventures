Heath Blondies

Original cookbook page 578

Ingredients

  • 11/4 c light brown sugar packed
  • 1/2 c granulated sugar
  • 1c butter (2 sticks) melted
  • 2 eggs large
  • 1egg yolk large
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • e 21/4 c all purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1tsp salt
  • 2 tsp corn starch
  • 8 0z bag Heath toffee bits

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. To a bowl mix melted butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, vanilla, eggs and egg yolk.
  3. Ina separate bowl sift together flour, baking powder, corn starch and salt.
  4. Mix until fully combined.
  5. Measure one cup of the Heath toffee bits and add to the batter. Fold in.
  6. Line a 9x13 pan with parchment paper.
  7. Spread the thick batter into the pan evenly.
  8. Bake in the middle rack for 35 minutes uncovered.
  9. Remove and allow to cool for 30 minutes before cutting into 15 bars.
