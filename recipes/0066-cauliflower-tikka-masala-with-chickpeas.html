Cauliflower Tikka Masala with Chickpeas

Original cookbook page 66

A vegetarian riff on a popular Indian dish, swapping cauliflower and chickpeas for chicken. Serve over rice for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut or canola oil
  • 4 cups cauliflower florets
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 15-ounce can chickpeas, rinsed
  • 1½ cups tikka masala sauce
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add cauliflower and salt; cook, stirring occasionally, until lightly browned, about 3 minutes.
  3. Add water and cover; cook until tender, 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Add chickpeas and tikka masala sauce; cook, stirring, until hot, about 2 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and stir in butter.
  6. Serve topped with cilantro if desired.
