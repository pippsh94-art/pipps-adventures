Cauliflower Tikka Masala with Chickpeas

Original cookbook page 66

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add cauliflower florets and cook until lightly browned
  3. Add water and cover, steaming until cauliflower is tender, 5 to 5 minutes
  4. Add chickpeas and sauce, cook stirring occasionally until heated through
  5. Serve topped with cilantro and fresh lime juice if desired
