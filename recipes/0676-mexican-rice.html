Mexican Rice

Original cookbook page 676

Ingredients

  • 2 Mexican Rice

Instructions

  1. Now in a skillet heat little over 1/4 cup of oil until HOT now add 2 cups of long grain rice or jasmine rice.
  2. Fry until light Brown now add your mix from the blender, give it a good stir and then taste it to see if you need to add more salt or other seasonings.
  3. After you stir to combine the rice
  4. medium heat until rice has absorbed the broth, turn heat off leave covered for 5-10 minutes.
  5. Remove the cover fluff the rice and enjoy .
  6. We add the jalapeno just for flavor it's totally optional, we fight for it at my house 3 &
