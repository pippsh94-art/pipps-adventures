Mini Hasselback Potatoes - Damn

Original cookbook page 114

Ingredients

  • + 11/2 pounds petite gold potatoes
  • + 6 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
  • + Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • + 11/2 tablespoons minced fresh basil leaves
  • + 1 tablespoon minced fresh chives
  • + 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley leaves
  • + 1 clove garlic, finely grated

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. 2, Using a sharp knife, make crosswise cuts in each potato, about 1/8-inch apart, stopping about 1/4 inch from the bottom.
  2. Place potatoes in a single layer, cut side up, onto the prepared baking sheet. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons olive oil; season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. Place into oven and bake until tender and crisp, about 45-50 minutes.
  4. Ina small bowl, combine remaining tablespoons olive oil, basil, chives, parsley and garlic; season with salt and pepper, to taste,
