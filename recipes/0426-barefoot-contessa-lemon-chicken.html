Barefoot Contessa Lemon Chicken

Original cookbook page 426

Ingredients

  • V4 cup good olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons minced garlic (9 cloves)
  • 1/3 cup dry white wine
  • 1 tablespoon grated lemon zest (See the th
  • different ways to zest.)
  • 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lemon juic
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon minced fresh thyme leaves
  • Kosher salt and freshly ground black pepp
  • 4 boneless chicken breasts, skin on (6 to 8
  • each)
  • 1 whole lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Warm the olive oil in a small saucepan over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook for just 1 minute. Off the heat, add the white wine, lemon zest, lemon juice, oregano, thyme, and 1 teaspoon each of salt and pepper.
  3. Place the chicken breasts, skin side up, in a baking dish just large enough to hold them. Pour the oil mixture over the chicken.
  4. Slice the lemon and place a slice on each chicken breast.
  5. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes, depending on the size of the chicken breasts, until the chicken is done and the skin is lightly browned.
  6. Serve hot with the pan juices spooned over the top.
