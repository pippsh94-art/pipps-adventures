How To Cook Bacon In The Oven (Easy Method)

Original cookbook page 13

A simple, no-fuss method for cooking perfectly crispy bacon in the oven with minimal cleanup.

Prep: 5 minutes • Cook: 20 minutes • Total: 25 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces bacon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Cover baking sheet with parchment paper. Place bacon on parchment paper.
  3. Place in oven for 20 minutes or until bacon has reached desired crispiness.
  4. Remove from oven and place on paper towel for 1 minute to cool.
