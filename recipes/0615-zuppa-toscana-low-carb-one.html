Zuppa Toscana, Low Carb, One

Original cookbook page 615

Ingredients

  • * 8 ounces Ground sausage cooked
  • * 1 quart Beef broth
  • * 12 ounces Cauliflower frozen
  • * 1cup Kale
  • * 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • * 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • * 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • * 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • * 1/4 cup cream
  • * 4slices bacon

Instructions

  1. Set aside the cream and bacon to add at the end.
  2. Heat on medium heat for about 25 minutes, or until the cauliflower reaches thi desire.
