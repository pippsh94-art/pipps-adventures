Keto Flat Bread

Original cookbook page 653

Ingredients

  • 2 oz mozzarella
  • 1 cup parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  1. The recipe is 1 cup mozzarella,1 cup parmesan, 1 egg, mix well sprinkle garlic powder and Italian seasoning put parchment paper in the cookie sheet and spray with pam oven on at 350 degrees for 15 minutes.
  2. I used 12 minutes or until the edges start to brown, then turn the broiler on for 1 minute remove from oven and enjoy.
