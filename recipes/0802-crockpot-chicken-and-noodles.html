Crockpot Chicken And Noodles

Original cookbook page 802

Ingredients

  • 10 mins
  • 6 hrs
  • 6 hrs 10 mins

Instructions

  1. Add chicken, broth, and seasonings to crockpot
  2. Cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours
  3. During the last 30 minutes of cooking, add uncooked noodles
  4. Stir well and cook until noodles are tender
  5. Serve hot with optional garnishes
