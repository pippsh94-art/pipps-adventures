Heath Blondlies - Cook Like Lauren

Original cookbook page 593

Ingredients

  • * 1/2 c granulated sugar

  • * 1 egg yolk large
  • * 2 tsp vanilla
  • * 21/4 c all purpose flour
  • * 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • * 1tsp salt
