If you enjoy a good burger and like veal, having a veal burger |

Original cookbook page 280

Ingredients

  • 11/2 pounds ground veal
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, or margarine
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • Dash ground black pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 2tablespoons water
  • 1/2 cup fine, dry unseasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

  1. The ingredients come together to add flavor and juiciness to these veal patties.
  2. They're delicious served in buns along with your favorite burger fixings and french fries.
  3. Or you can serve them as patties with a side of noodles,
  4. roasted potatoes, and a green salad.
  5. 1/2 cup fine, dry unseasoned breadcrumbs
