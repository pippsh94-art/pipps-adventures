Pork Butt Instant Pot Instructions

Original cookbook page 262

Ingredients

  • Pork roast (pork butt)
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • Dry rub ingredients (salt, pepper, spices)
  • Chicken broth
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Liquid smoke
  • BBQ sauce of your choice

Instructions

  1. Start by trimming the fat from your pork roast and cut it into 4 same-size chunks.
  2. Now, want you to add all the dry ingredients to a large bowl and whisk them together.
  3. Then add your pork roast chunks to rub them with the mix and coat them well.
  4. Next, add 2 teaspoons of olive oil to the Instant Pot and set it to saute.
  5. Once it indicates hot, go ahead and add 2 pieces of the pork and sear the 2 pieces on each side for about 2 minutes each.
  6. At this point remove your pork and set it aside on a plate and repeat the process with your remaining pork pieces.
  7. Once your pork is seared and removed from the pot, press cancels then add half a cup of chicken broth.
  8. With the use of a wooden spoon deglaze the bottom of the pot, and make sure to scrape up all the bits.
  9. Then add the remaining chicken broth with Worcestershire sauce and liquid smoke.
  10. Now you want to place your pork chunks directly into the liquid in the pot, and try to space them as best possible.
  11. Then secure the lid and make sure the vent is set to sealing, let them pressure cook/manual on high pressure for about 60 minutes.
  12. Then let the pressure release naturally for 20 minutes or until the drops.
  13. Now, remove the pork to a large bowl and shred it with 2 forks then add your favorite BBQ sauce of your choice.
