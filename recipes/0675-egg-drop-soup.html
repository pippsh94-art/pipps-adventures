Egg Drop Soup

Original cookbook page 675

Ingredients

  • 32 oz chicken broth
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 oz sliced green onions

Instructions

  1. The ingredients are just 3202's of chicken broth, 1/2 tbsp of turmeric, 1 tsp salt, 1 tsp white pepper, 3 eggs, 1 cup corn, 1 tbsp of cornstarch in 1 tbsp of water, 1 tbsp of sesame oil and 10z sliced green onions.
  2. I like to add a few drops of soy sauce when serving.
