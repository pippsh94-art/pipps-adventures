Keto Breakfast Pinwheels and Feta

Original cookbook page 82

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Mozzarella cheese shredded
  • 2 oz garlic & herb cream cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1 tbsp oil for egg wash

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Lay out spinach and herb wraps
  3. Spread a thin layer of cream cheese filling on each wrap
  4. Layer with bacon slices, feta cheese, and spinach
  5. Roll tightly and place seam-side down on a baking sheet
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until edges are lightly crispy
  7. Cool slightly before serving
