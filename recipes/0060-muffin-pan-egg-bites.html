Muffin-Pan Egg Bites

These portable egg bites are perfect for meal prep. You can make them ahead and reheat throughout the week for quick breakfasts.

Prep: 15 minutes • Cook: 18 minutes • Total: 33 minutes

Ingredients

  • Cooking spray
  • 8 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh chives

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Coat a 12-cup muffin tin with cooking spray.
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper in a large bowl until well combined.
  3. Divide cheese, scallions, bell pepper and chives evenly among the muffin cups.
  4. Pour the egg mixture evenly over the vegetables in each cup, filling about 3/4 full.
  5. Bake until the eggs are set and lightly golden on top, 16 to 18 minutes.
  6. Let cool in the pan for 2 minutes before removing. Serve warm or store in the refrigerator for up to 4 days.
