Cream cheese frosting for carrot cake (continuation from previous page)

Ingredients

  • 3½ - 4 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 stick butter or margarine (softened)
  • 1 - 8oz block cream cheese (softened)

Instructions

  1. Mix well & spread on cooled carrot cake
