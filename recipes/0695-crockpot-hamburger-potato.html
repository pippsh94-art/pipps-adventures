Crockpot Hamburger Potato

Original cookbook page 695

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 4 cups potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • onion, chopped
  • 1can (10.5 oz) condensed cream of mushroom soup.
  • 1cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • '1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: chopped fresh parsley for garnish
  • Brown the ground beef in a skillet over medium heat until fully cooked.
  • Drain excess grease.
  • In a bowl, mix together the cooked ground beef, sliced potatoes,
  • chopped onion, condensed cream of mushroom soup, shredded
  • cheddar cheese, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper.
  • Transfer the mixture to a greased crockpot.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 6-8 hours or until the potatoes are
  • tender.
  • Serve hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley if desired.
  • Enjoy the comforting and hearty Crockpot Hamburger Potato
