Keto Bread

Original cookbook page 3

A simple low-carb keto bread made with mozzarella and almond flour.

Cook: 15 mins • Total: 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mozzarella
  • 1/4 almond flour
  • 1 egg
  • garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Combine 1 cup mozzarella, 1/4 cup almond flour, 1 egg, and garlic powder.
  2. Bake at 350°F for 15 minutes.
