Lemonade (Chick-fil-a)

Original cookbook page 446

Ingredients

  • 6 servings 10 minutes
  • 142 kcal
  • 11/2 cup freshly squeezed
  • lemon juice (about 7 to 8
  • large lemons)
  • I] cup granulated sugar
  • 5 cups water

Instructions

  1. Squeeze lemons to get 1 1/2 cups of fresh lemon juice.
  2. In a large pitcher, combine the lemon juice and sugar. Stir until sugar is dissolved.
  3. Add 5 cups of water and stir well.
  4. Serve over ice.
