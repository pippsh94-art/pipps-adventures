Keto Seafood Cioppino

Italian-American seafood stew with tomatoes, wine, and fresh herbs. A celebration of the ocean's bounty in every bowl.

#0138

⏰ Timing

Prep: 25 minutes
Cook: 35 minutes
Total: 1 hour

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound firm white fish, cubed
  • 1/2 pound large shrimp, peeled
  • 1/2 pound scallops
  • 1 pound mussels, cleaned
  • 1/2 pound crab legs, cracked
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 2 cups seafood stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tablespoons fresh oregano
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Red pepper flakes to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and bell pepper. Cook for 6-8 minutes until softened.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in white wine and let simmer for 3 minutes to reduce alcohol.
  5. Add crushed tomatoes, seafood stock, bay leaves, and oregano.
  6. Bring to a simmer and cook for 15 minutes to develop flavors.
  7. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.
  8. Add firm fish and scallops first, simmer for 3 minutes.
  9. Add shrimp and cook for 2 minutes.
  10. Finally add mussels and crab legs, cover and cook for 5 minutes until mussels open.
  11. Discard any unopened mussels.
  12. Remove bay leaves and taste for seasoning.
  13. Ladle into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley.
  14. Serve with keto bread or eat as a hearty soup.
  15. Best enjoyed immediately while seafood is perfectly tender.
