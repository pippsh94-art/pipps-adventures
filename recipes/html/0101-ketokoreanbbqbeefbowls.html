Keto Korean BBQ Beef Bowls

Tender marinated beef with Asian flavors served over cauliflower rice. Bold, savory, and satisfying Korean-inspired meal.

#0101

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 15 minutes
Total: 35 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pounds thinly sliced ribeye or sirloin
  • 4 cups cauliflower rice
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon erythritol
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 4 green onions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • 4 soft-boiled eggs
  • Kimchi for serving (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together coconut aminos, sesame oil, rice vinegar, erythritol, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes.
  2. Add sliced beef to marinade and let sit for 15 minutes.
  3. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet or wok over high heat.
  4. Remove beef from marinade (reserve marinade) and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until cooked through.
  5. Pour reserved marinade into the pan and cook for 2 minutes until sauce thickens.
  6. Meanwhile, sauté cauliflower rice in a separate pan for 5-7 minutes until tender.
  7. Divide cauliflower rice among bowls.
  8. Top with Korean BBQ beef and any pan sauce.
  9. Garnish with green onions, sesame seeds, and julienned cucumber.
  10. Add a soft-boiled egg to each bowl.
  11. Serve with kimchi on the side if desired.
