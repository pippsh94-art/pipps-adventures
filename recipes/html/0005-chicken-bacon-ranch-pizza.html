Chicken Bacon Ranch Pizza

A low carb chicken bacon ranch pizza with a ground chicken crust, homemade ranch topping, mozzarella cheese, and bacon.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground chicken (crust)
  • 1/2 c mozzarella (crust)
  • 1 c sour cream (ranch)
  • 1 tbsp parsley, chopped (ranch)
  • 1 tsp dill weed (ranch)
  • 1 tsp chives (ranch)
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder (ranch)
  • 1 tbsp onion powder (ranch)
  • 1 tsp salt (ranch)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper (ranch)
  • 1 c mozzarella cheese, shredded (topping)
  • 2 bacon slices, diced (topping)

Instructions

  1. Make crust using ground chicken and mozzarella
  2. Prepare ranch topping by mixing sour cream with herbs and spices
  3. Top crust with ranch mixture, mozzarella cheese, and bacon
  4. Bake until golden and cheese is melted
