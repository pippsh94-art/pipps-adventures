Keto Moroccan Lamb Tagine

Aromatic lamb stew with warm spices, olives, and preserved lemons. Exotic North African flavors in a comforting dish.

#0105

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 1 hour 30 minutes
Total: 1 hour 50 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 3 pounds lamb shoulder, cut into chunks
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup green olives
  • 1/4 cup preserved lemons, chopped (or lemon zest)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Almonds for garnish (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Season lamb chunks with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  3. Brown lamb pieces on all sides, working in batches. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add sliced onions to the same pot and cook for 5-6 minutes until softened.
  5. Add garlic, ginger, and all spices. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  6. Return lamb to the pot and add beef broth.
  7. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low.
  8. Cover and simmer for 1 hour 15 minutes until lamb is tender.
  9. Add green olives and preserved lemons.
  10. Simmer uncovered for 15 more minutes until sauce thickens.
  11. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  12. Garnish with fresh cilantro and almonds if using.
  13. Serve hot over cauliflower rice or alone.
