Keto Maasai Warrior Blood Sausage

Traditional East African protein-rich sausage made keto-friendly. Ancient warrior nutrition adapted for modern ketogenic living.

#0172

⏰ Timing

Prep: 45 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 1 hour 10 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pounds grass-fed ground beef
  • 1 pound beef liver, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup beef blood (or beef stock concentrate)
  • 2 large onions, finely minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons coriander seeds, ground
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon cardamom
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Natural sausage casings (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • Traditional African vegetables for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. If using casings, soak them in warm water to soften.
  2. In a large bowl, combine ground beef and chopped liver.
  3. Add beef blood (or concentrated stock) to bind the mixture.
  4. Mix in minced onions, garlic, and all spices.
  5. Knead mixture thoroughly with hands until well combined.
  6. The mixture should hold together but not be too wet.
  7. If using casings, stuff mixture into casings and tie off in 6-inch links.
  8. If not using casings, form into thick patties or logs.
  9. Let sausages rest for 30 minutes to develop flavors.
  10. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  11. Cook sausages slowly, turning regularly, for 20-25 minutes.
  12. They should be browned on all sides and cooked through.
  13. Internal temperature should reach 160°F.
  14. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  15. Traditionally served with fermented milk and vegetables.
  16. This recipe honors the nutritional wisdom of the Maasai people.
