Keto Opera Cake

Elegant French layer cake with almond sponge, chocolate ganache, and coffee buttercream. The ultimate keto celebration dessert.

#0140

⏰ Timing

Prep: 2 hours
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 6 hours (including chilling)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs, separated
  • 1/2 cup powdered erythritol, divided
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 8 ounces sugar-free dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 1/4 cup strong espresso, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons powdered erythritol
  • Gold leaf for decoration (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Line a 15x10 inch jelly roll pan with parchment.
  2. Beat egg yolks with 1/4 cup erythritol until pale and thick.
  3. In a separate bowl, whip egg whites to soft peaks, gradually add remaining erythritol until stiff peaks form.
  4. Fold almond flour, cocoa, and salt into egg yolk mixture.
  5. Gently fold in 1/3 of egg whites, then remaining whites in two additions.
  6. Spread batter evenly in prepared pan and bake 12-15 minutes until springy.
  7. Cool completely, then cut into 3 equal rectangles.
  8. For ganache: Heat 1/2 cup cream and pour over chocolate. Stir until smooth. Cool until spreadable.
  9. For buttercream: Beat butter until fluffy. Add espresso, liqueur, vanilla, and 2 tablespoons erythritol.
  10. Assembly: Place one cake layer on serving plate. Brush with espresso mixture.
  11. Spread half the buttercream over cake. Top with second layer.
  12. Spread ganache over second layer. Top with final cake layer.
  13. Spread remaining buttercream on top. Refrigerate 4 hours until set.
  14. Trim edges for clean lines. Decorate with gold leaf if desired.
  15. Slice with a sharp knife for elegant presentation.
