Keto Georgian Khachapuri

Cheese-filled bread boat from the Caucasus. Georgian comfort food that's both rustic and elegant, made keto-friendly.

#0165

⏰ Timing

Prep: 45 minutes
Cook: 20 minutes
Total: 1 hour 5 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup psyllium husk powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 16 ounces fresh mozzarella, grated
  • 8 ounces Georgian cheese (or additional mozzarella)
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Fresh herbs for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  2. Mix almond flour, psyllium husk, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat 3 eggs with warm water and olive oil.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients to form a soft dough.
  5. Let dough rest for 10 minutes to hydrate.
  6. Divide dough into 4 portions for individual boats.
  7. Roll each portion into oval shapes on parchment paper.
  8. Form raised edges by pinching and rolling the sides up.
  9. Create boat shapes with pointed ends.
  10. For filling: Mix grated cheeses with softened cream cheese.
  11. Fill each boat with cheese mixture, leaving wells in center.
  12. Bake for 15 minutes until edges are golden and cheese is bubbling.
  13. Remove from oven and crack an egg yolk into each cheese well.
  14. Add a pat of butter to each.
  15. Return to oven for 3-4 minutes until egg whites are just set.
  16. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve immediately while hot.
