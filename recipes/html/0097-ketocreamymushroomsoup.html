Keto Creamy Mushroom Soup

Rich, velvety mushroom soup that's completely dairy-based and satisfying. Perfect for cold days or as an elegant starter.

#0097

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 30 minutes
Total: 45 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds mixed mushrooms, sliced (button, cremini, shiitake)
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup dry sherry or white wine
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Chives for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat butter in a large pot over medium-high heat.
  2. Add sliced mushrooms and cook for 8-10 minutes until golden brown.
  3. Add diced onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in sherry or wine and scrape up any browned bits.
  6. Add chicken broth and bring to a simmer.
  7. Simmer for 15 minutes to develop flavors.
  8. Using an immersion blender, blend about half the soup for texture (optional).
  9. Stir in heavy cream and cream cheese until smooth.
  10. Add fresh thyme, salt, and pepper.
  11. Simmer for 5 more minutes until heated through.
  12. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
  13. Ladle into bowls and garnish with fresh parsley and chives.
