Keto Buffalo Chicken Lettuce Wraps

Spicy buffalo chicken salad served in crisp lettuce cups. Perfect for game day or a light, satisfying lunch.

#0107

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 20 minutes
Total: 35 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce, divided
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 celery stalks, finely diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup blue cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, chopped
  • 1 head butter lettuce, leaves separated
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Blue cheese dressing for serving
  • Celery sticks for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Cook chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until cooked through (165°F internal temp).
  4. Let chicken cool, then shred into bite-sized pieces.
  5. Toss shredded chicken with 1/4 cup buffalo sauce.
  6. In a large bowl, mix mayonnaise, sour cream, and remaining buffalo sauce.
  7. Add shredded chicken, diced celery, red onion, blue cheese, and chives.
  8. Gently fold everything together.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Chill mixture for 15 minutes to let flavors meld.
  11. Spoon buffalo chicken mixture into lettuce cups.
  12. Serve with blue cheese dressing and celery sticks on the side.
