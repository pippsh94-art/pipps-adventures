Keto Eocene Greenhouse World Tropical Feast

Earth at its warmest - palm trees in the Arctic and crocodiles at the poles. A greenhouse world feast of tropical abundance.

#0210

⏰ Timing

Prep: Eocene thermal maximum
Cook: Greenhouse cooking conditions
Total: 56-34 million years ago

🛒 Ingredients

  • Arctic palm tree proteins
  • Antarctic crocodile seasonings
  • Hyracotherium dawn horse meat
  • Ambulocetus walking whale proteins
  • Gastornis terror bird essence
  • Tropical greenhouse vegetables
  • High CO2 atmosphere cooking gas
  • Warm shallow sea minerals
  • Rainforest canopy spices
  • Global tropical abundance

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Create feast using arctic palm tree proteins from when the poles were tropical.
  2. Season with antarctic crocodile compounds from polar reptiles.
  3. Include hyracotherium dawn horse meat from early horse evolution.
  4. Add ambulocetus walking whale proteins from whale terrestrial ancestors.
  5. Use gastornis terror bird essence from flightless predatory birds.
  6. Incorporate tropical greenhouse vegetables that grew globally.
  7. Cook in high CO2 atmosphere that created the greenhouse effect.
  8. Use warm shallow sea minerals from the globally flooded continents.
  9. Season with rainforest canopy spices from forests that covered most land.
  10. Everything should be abundant, lush, and incredibly diverse.
  11. Cook at temperatures much higher than modern Earth.
  12. No ice caps existed - even Antarctica was warm and forested.
  13. The dish should overflow with tropical richness and variety.
  14. Represent the most biodiverse and warm period in mammalian history.
  15. Serve in steaming abundance representing global greenhouse conditions.
  16. This feast shows Earth at maximum warmth and biological productivity.
  17. A glimpse of what Earth might become again with climate change.
