Keto Oligocene Cooling Global Transition Soup

Earth transitioning from greenhouse to icehouse. The great cooling that ended tropical dominance and began the modern world.

#0211

⏰ Timing

Prep: 34-23 million years ago
Cook: Global cooling process
Total: Oligocene epoch transition

🛒 Ingredients

  • Antarctic ice sheet formation
  • Falling CO2 atmospheric compounds
  • Grassland expansion proteins
  • Forest retreat essences
  • Whale fully aquatic evolution
  • Primate brain development spices
  • Cooling ocean current minerals
  • Seasonal climate establishment
  • Mammalian size reduction factors
  • Modern ecosystem precursors

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Begin with antarctic ice sheet formation representing the end of greenhouse Earth.
  2. Add falling CO2 atmospheric compounds that triggered global cooling.
  3. Include grassland expansion proteins as forests retreated globally.
  4. Use forest retreat essences showing the end of global tropical conditions.
  5. Add whale fully aquatic evolution proteins from complete ocean adaptation.
  6. Season with primate brain development spices showing increased intelligence.
  7. Include cooling ocean current minerals that enhanced global cooling.
  8. Add seasonal climate establishment compounds creating distinct seasons.
  9. Use mammalian size reduction factors as cooling favored smaller bodies.
  10. Include modern ecosystem precursors showing emergence of familiar patterns.
  11. Cook at steadily decreasing temperatures throughout preparation.
  12. The soup should transition from tropical richness to temperate complexity.
  13. Grassland flavors become increasingly prominent.
  14. Forest flavors become more concentrated and specialized.
  15. Serve at moderate temperature representing the new cooler world.
  16. This soup bridges the ancient warm world with the modern cool one.
  17. The beginning of the world we recognize today.
