Keto Avocado Egg Salad

Creamy egg salad made with avocado instead of mayo. Perfect for lettuce wraps, keto sandwiches, or eating straight up.

#0049

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 12 minutes
Total: 27 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 8 large eggs
    • \n
  • 2 ripe avocados
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
    • \n
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
    • \n
  • 3 green onions, chopped
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, chopped
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    • \n
  • Paprika for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Place eggs in a large pot and cover with cold water by 1 inch.
    2. \n
  2. Bring water to a boil, then remove from heat, cover, and let sit for 12 minutes.
    3. \n
  3. Transfer eggs to ice water bath and let cool completely.
    4. \n
  4. Peel eggs and roughly chop them into bite-sized pieces.
    5. \n
  5. In a large bowl, mash avocados with lemon juice until mostly smooth.
    6. \n
  6. Stir in Dijon mustard, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
    7. \n
  7. Gently fold in chopped eggs, celery, green onions, and chives.
    8. \n
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
    9. \n
  9. Sprinkle with paprika before serving.
    10. \n
  10. Serve immediately or chill for up to 2 days.
