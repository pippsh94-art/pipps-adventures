🌟 Keto New Zealand Pavlova

Ethereal meringue dessert with cream and berries. A magnificent finale to our monumental 170-recipe journey - pure Kiwi elegance.

#0170

⏰ Timing

Prep: 45 minutes
Cook: 1 hour 30 minutes
Total: 4 hours (including cooling)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 6 large egg whites, at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered erythritol
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch (or arrowroot)
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered erythritol (for cream)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (for cream)
  • 2 cups mixed berries (strawberries, kiwi, passion fruit)
  • Fresh mint for garnish
  • Edible flowers (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line baking sheet with parchment.
  2. Draw a 9-inch circle on parchment as a guide.
  3. In a spotlessly clean bowl, whip egg whites until soft peaks form.
  4. Gradually add erythritol, one tablespoon at a time, whipping well.
  5. Continue whipping until meringue is thick, glossy, and holds stiff peaks.
  6. Whisk in vanilla, vinegar, and cornstarch until just combined.
  7. Spoon meringue onto parchment circle.
  8. Create a shallow well in center with slightly raised edges.
  9. Place in oven and immediately reduce temperature to 225°F.
  10. Bake for 1 hour 15 minutes until crisp outside but soft inside.
  11. Turn off oven and let cool completely with door ajar.
  12. For topping: Whip cream with erythritol and vanilla until soft peaks form.
  13. Just before serving, spoon cream into meringue well.
  14. Top with fresh berries arranged artistically.
  15. Garnish with mint and edible flowers if using.
  16. Serve immediately - this is the crown jewel of 170 recipes!
