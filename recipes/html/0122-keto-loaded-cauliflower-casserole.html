Keto Loaded Cauliflower Casserole

All the flavors of loaded baked potatoes in a creamy cauliflower casserole. Comfort food that's completely keto-friendly.

#0122

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 35 minutes
Total: 55 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 large heads cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded, divided
  • 4 green onions, chopped, divided
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • Extra sour cream for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Steam cauliflower florets for 12-15 minutes until very tender.
  3. Drain well and let cool slightly.
  4. Cook bacon until crispy, then crumble. Reserve 2 tablespoons for topping.
  5. In a large bowl, mash cauliflower with butter until mostly smooth.
  6. Beat in cream cheese, sour cream, heavy cream, and garlic until creamy.
  7. Fold in 1.5 cups cheddar cheese, most of the bacon, and half the green onions.
  8. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika.
  9. Transfer to prepared baking dish.
  10. Top with remaining cheddar cheese and reserved bacon.
  11. Bake for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
  12. Garnish with remaining green onions.
  13. Serve hot with extra sour cream on the side.
