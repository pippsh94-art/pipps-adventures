Keto Cauliflower Gratin

Rich and creamy cauliflower gratin with multiple cheeses and herbs. The perfect side dish for any keto meal.

#0070

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 35 minutes
Total: 55 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 large heads cauliflower, cut into florets
    • \n
  • 2 cups heavy cream
    • \n
  • 4 tablespoons butter
    • \n
  • 3 tablespoons almond flour
    • \n
  • 1 cup Gruyère cheese, grated
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
    • \n
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chives, chopped

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F and butter a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    2. \n
  2. Steam cauliflower florets for 8-10 minutes until just tender. Drain well.
    3. \n
  3. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    4. \n
  4. Whisk in almond flour and cook for 1 minute.
    5. \n
  5. Gradually whisk in heavy cream, cooking until thickened.
    6. \n
  6. Add garlic, thyme, nutmeg, salt, and pepper.
    7. \n
  7. Stir in half the Gruyère and all the Parmesan until melted.
    8. \n
  8. Add steamed cauliflower to the cheese sauce and gently toss.
    9. \n
  9. Transfer to prepared baking dish.
    10. \n
  10. Top with remaining Gruyère and cheddar cheese.
    11. \n
  11. Bake for 25-30 minutes until golden and bubbly.
    12. \n
  12. Let cool for 10 minutes, then garnish with fresh chives.
    13. \n
  13. Serve as a side dish with your favorite protein.
