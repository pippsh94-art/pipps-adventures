Keto Mushroom Wellington

Elegant mushroom and herb filling wrapped in keto pastry. A stunning vegetarian centerpiece for special occasions.

#0136

⏰ Timing

Prep: 45 minutes
Cook: 35 minutes
Total: 1 hour 20 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup butter, cold
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 2 pounds mixed mushrooms, chopped
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme
  • 1/4 cup dry sherry
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 ounces goat cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Egg wash for brushing

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. For pastry: Mix almond flour and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Cut in cream cheese and butter until mixture resembles crumbs.
  2. Add beaten egg and mix until dough forms. Wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add mushrooms and cook for 10-12 minutes until moisture evaporates.
  5. Add onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  6. Add garlic, thyme, remaining salt, and pepper. Cook for 1 minute.
  7. Pour in sherry and cook until evaporated.
  8. Stir in walnuts and parsley. Let cool completely.
  9. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  10. Roll pastry between parchment paper into a large rectangle.
  11. Spread goat cheese down the center of pastry.
  12. Top with mushroom mixture.
  13. Fold pastry over filling and seal edges with egg wash.
  14. Place seam-side down on baking sheet and brush with egg wash.
  15. Bake for 30-35 minutes until golden brown.
  16. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
