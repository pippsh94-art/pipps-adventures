Keto Vietnamese Pho with Shirataki Noodles

Aromatic Vietnamese pho with rich bone broth and shirataki noodles. All the authentic flavors in a keto-friendly bowl.

#0147

⏰ Timing

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 4 hours
Total: 4 hours 30 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef bones
  • 2 pounds beef chuck roast
  • 1 large onion, halved
  • 4-inch piece ginger, halved lengthwise
  • 6 star anise pods
  • 4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1 tablespoon coriander seeds
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 pound shirataki noodles, rinsed
  • 1/2 pound raw beef sirloin, thinly sliced
  • Bean sprouts, cilantro, Thai basil, lime wedges for serving
  • Sliced jalapeños and onions for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Char onion and ginger over open flame or under broiler until blackened in spots.
  2. Toast star anise, cloves, cinnamon, coriander, and fennel in a dry pan until fragrant.
  3. Place beef bones in a large pot and cover with water.
  4. Bring to a boil, then drain and rinse bones (removes impurities).
  5. Return bones to pot with chuck roast, charred onion and ginger, and toasted spices.
  6. Add 4 quarts water and bring to a gentle simmer.
  7. Simmer for 3-4 hours, skimming foam regularly.
  8. Remove chuck roast after 2 hours when tender. Shred and set aside.
  9. Strain broth through fine-mesh sieve and season with fish sauce and salt.
  10. Prepare shirataki noodles according to package directions.
  11. To serve: Place noodles in bowls, top with raw beef slices and shredded chuck.
  12. Ladle hot broth over to cook raw beef instantly.
  13. Serve with bean sprouts, herbs, lime wedges, and jalapeños.
  14. Let diners customize with toppings and seasonings.
