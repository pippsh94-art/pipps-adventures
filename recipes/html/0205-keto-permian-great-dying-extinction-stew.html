Keto Permian Great Dying Extinction Stew

A somber memorial stew representing the greatest mass extinction event. Flavors of the 96% that didn't survive.

#0205

⏰ Timing

Prep: 252 million years ago
Cook: The Great Dying duration
Total: End Permian catastrophe

🛒 Ingredients

  • Siberian Traps volcanic proteins
  • Oceanic anoxia flavor compounds
  • Greenhouse gas seasonings
  • Acid rain marinades
  • Methane hydrate releases
  • Gorgonopsid predator meat
  • Glossopteris forest remains
  • Rugose coral extinctions
  • Trilobite final flavors
  • Survival essence concentrates

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Begin with proteins from the Siberian Traps volcanic event.
  2. These massive eruptions triggered the greatest extinction in Earth's history.
  3. Add oceanic anoxia compounds representing oxygen-depleted seas.
  4. Season with greenhouse gases that caused catastrophic warming.
  5. Include acid rain marinades that destroyed terrestrial ecosystems.
  6. Add methane hydrate releases that amplified the catastrophe.
  7. Use gorgonopsid predator meat from the dominant Permian hunters.
  8. Include remnants of glossopteris forests that covered Gondwana.
  9. Honor rugose corals that vanished forever in this event.
  10. Add the final flavors of trilobites after 270 million years of success.
  11. This stew represents the loss of 96% of marine species.
  12. 83% of terrestrial species also perished in this catastrophe.
  13. Cook slowly to represent the gradual ecological collapse.
  14. The few survival essence concentrates represent the 4% that lived.
  15. Serve as a memorial to the greatest loss of biodiversity ever.
  16. This stew reminds us that extinction is always possible.
  17. From this catastrophe, dinosaurs would eventually rise.
