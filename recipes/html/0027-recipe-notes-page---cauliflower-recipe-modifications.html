Ingredients

  • Additional modifications to previous recipe:
  • Extra 1/2 to 1 cup more cauliflower
  • Extra 1/2 cup or less water
  • Omit xanthan gum

Instructions

  1. These are modification notes for a previous recipe:
  2. Add more cauliflower (1/2c to 1c more than original).
  3. Add more water (maybe 1/2c or less).
  4. Don't use xanthan gum.
  5. Result: Slightly less rich but still tastes great.
