Keto Canadian Tourtière

Traditional Quebec meat pie made keto-friendly. French-Canadian heritage in every savory, spiced bite of this holiday classic.

#0164

⏰ Timing

Prep: 45 minutes
Cook: 40 minutes
Total: 1 hour 25 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 3 cups almond flour
  • 1/2 cup cold butter, cubed
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 pounds ground pork
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground sage
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon allspice
  • 1/4 teaspoon cloves
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Egg wash for top

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. For pastry: Mix almond flour and salt. Cut in butter and cream cheese until crumbly.
  2. Add egg and mix until dough forms. Wrap and chill for 30 minutes.
  3. For filling: Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Cook ground pork and beef until browned, breaking it up.
  5. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  6. Add garlic, sage, thyme, allspice, cloves, and bay leaf.
  7. Cook for 2 minutes until fragrant.
  8. Add beef broth and simmer for 15 minutes until mixture thickens.
  9. Remove bay leaf and season with salt and pepper. Cool completely.
  10. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  11. Divide pastry in half. Roll out bottom crust and place in 9-inch pie pan.
  12. Fill with cooled meat mixture.
  13. Roll out top crust and place over filling.
  14. Trim and crimp edges to seal.
  15. Cut vents in top crust and brush with egg wash.
  16. Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden brown.
  17. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
