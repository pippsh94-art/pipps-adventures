Keto Crab-Stuffed Avocados

Fresh avocado halves filled with a delicious crab salad mixture. Light, elegant, and perfect for a special lunch or appetizer.

#0074

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 0 minutes
Total: 15 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 3 large ripe avocados, halved and pitted
    • \n
  • 1 pound fresh crab meat, picked over
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, chopped
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
    • \n
  • 1 celery stalk, finely diced
    • \n
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
    • \n
  • Paprika for garnish
    • \n
  • Lemon wedges for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Halve avocados and remove pits, leaving a small well for stuffing.
    2. \n
  2. Brush cut surfaces with lemon juice to prevent browning.
    3. \n
  3. In a large bowl, combine crab meat, mayonnaise, sour cream, and remaining lemon juice.
    4. \n
  4. Gently fold in chives, dill, celery, and green onion.
    5. \n
  5. Season with Old Bay, salt, and cayenne pepper.
    6. \n
  6. Mix gently to avoid breaking up the crab meat.
    7. \n
  7. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed.
    8. \n
  8. Divide crab mixture evenly among avocado halves.
    9. \n
  9. Sprinkle with paprika for color.
    10. \n
  10. Serve immediately with lemon wedges.
    11. \n
  11. Can be chilled for up to 2 hours before serving.
