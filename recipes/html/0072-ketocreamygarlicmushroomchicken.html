Keto Creamy Garlic Mushroom Chicken

Tender chicken in a rich, creamy garlic mushroom sauce. Restaurant-quality flavor made at home in one skillet.

#0072

⏰ Timing

Prep: 10 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 35 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • \n
  • 8 ounces baby bella mushrooms, sliced
    • \n
  • 4 tablespoons butter, divided
    • \n
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 1 cup heavy cream
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
    2. \n
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    3. \n
  3. Sear chicken breasts for 6-7 minutes per side until golden brown and cooked through.
    4. \n
  4. Remove chicken to a plate and tent with foil.
    5. \n
  5. In the same skillet, melt 2 tablespoons butter.
    6. \n
  6. Add sliced mushrooms and cook for 5-6 minutes until golden brown.
    7. \n
  7. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
    8. \n
  8. Pour in chicken broth and scrape up any browned bits.
    9. \n
  9. Stir in heavy cream and bring to a simmer.
    10. \n
  10. Add Parmesan cheese, thyme, and remaining butter, stirring until melted.
    11. \n
  11. Season sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
    12. \n
  12. Return chicken to skillet and spoon sauce over top.
    13. \n
  13. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
