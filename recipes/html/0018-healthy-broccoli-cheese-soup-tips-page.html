Healthy Broccoli Cheese Soup (Tips Page)

Original cookbook page 18

Tips and customization options for the 5-ingredient broccoli cheese soup. Includes suggestions for add-ins and preparation notes.

Ingredients

  • Base recipe uses 5 ingredients (see main recipe page)
  • Optional add-ins: Italian seasoning, cauliflower, shredded chicken, cooked bacon, sun-dried tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Go easy on anything with added salt like bacon, as the soup is already salty from cheese and broth.
  2. Add any other ingredients after the cheese melts first to prevent sticking issues.
  3. Customize with favorite spices, vegetables, or proteins as desired.
