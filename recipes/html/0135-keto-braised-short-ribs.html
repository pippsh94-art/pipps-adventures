Keto Braised Short Ribs

Fall-off-the-bone beef short ribs braised in red wine and herbs. Rich, comforting, and perfect for cold weather.

#0135

⏰ Timing

Prep: 25 minutes
Cook: 3 hours
Total: 3 hours 25 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 pounds beef short ribs, bone-in
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 bottle (750ml) red wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Season short ribs generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  4. Brown short ribs on all sides, about 4 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
  5. Add onion, carrots, and celery to the same pot. Cook for 8 minutes until softened.
  6. Add garlic and tomato paste. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  7. Pour in red wine and scrape up any browned bits.
  8. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes to reduce alcohol.
  9. Add beef broth, thyme, bay leaves, and rosemary.
  10. Return short ribs to pot, ensuring liquid comes 2/3 up the sides.
  11. Cover and braise in oven for 2.5-3 hours until meat falls off the bone.
  12. Remove ribs and strain braising liquid through fine-mesh sieve.
  13. In a saucepan, simmer strained liquid until reduced by half.
  14. Whisk in butter to finish the sauce.
  15. Serve ribs with reduced sauce and garnish with fresh parsley.
