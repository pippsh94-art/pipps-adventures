Keto Korean BBQ Ribs

Tender ribs with sweet and savory Korean flavors. Marinated in gochujang and served with pickled vegetables.

#0137

⏰ Timing

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 2 hours 30 minutes
Total: 3 hours

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 pounds pork ribs, cut into individual ribs
  • 1/4 cup coconut aminos
  • 3 tablespoons gochujang paste
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 3 tablespoons erythritol
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 Asian pear, grated
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon erythritol
  • Cilantro for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. For marinade: Whisk together coconut aminos, gochujang, sesame oil, 3 tablespoons erythritol, garlic, ginger, rice vinegar, and grated pear.
  2. Place ribs in a large bowl or zip-top bag and pour marinade over. Marinate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  3. For pickled cucumber: Toss cucumber slices with salt and let sit 15 minutes. Rinse and squeeze dry.
  4. Mix with rice vinegar and 1 teaspoon erythritol. Set aside.
  5. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  6. Remove ribs from marinade and place on a baking sheet lined with foil.
  7. Reserve marinade for basting.
  8. Bake for 2 hours, basting every 30 minutes with reserved marinade.
  9. Increase temperature to 425°F for last 10 minutes to caramelize.
  10. Let rest for 5 minutes.
  11. Garnish with green onions, sesame seeds, and cilantro.
  12. Serve with pickled cucumber and steamed vegetables.
  13. Can also be finished on a hot grill for extra char.
