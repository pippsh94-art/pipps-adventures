Keto Beef and Broccoli Stir Fry

A quick and easy Asian-inspired stir fry with tender beef, crisp broccoli, and a savory low-carb sauce.

#0837

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 10 minutes
Total: 25 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, sliced thin
    • \n
  • 4 cups broccoli florets
    • \n
  • 3 tablespoons avocado oil, divided
    • \n
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce (or coconut aminos)
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon erythritol
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
    • \n
  • 2 green onions, sliced
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
    2. \n
  2. Add beef strips and cook for 2-3 minutes until browned. Remove and set aside.
    3. \n
  3. Add remaining oil to the pan, then add broccoli florets.
    4. \n
  4. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until bright green and crisp-tender.
    5. \n
  5. Add garlic and ginger, cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
    6. \n
  6. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, erythritol, and red pepper flakes.
    7. \n
  7. Return beef to the pan and pour sauce over everything.
    8. \n
  8. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until heated through.
    9. \n
  9. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds before serving.
