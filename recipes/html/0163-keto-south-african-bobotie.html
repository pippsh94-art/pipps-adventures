Keto South African Bobotie

Spiced ground meat casserole with egg custard topping. Cape Malay cuisine that tells the story of South African culinary fusion.

#0163

⏰ Timing

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 45 minutes
Total: 1 hour 15 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground lamb and beef mix
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons mild curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup almonds, chopped
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional for keto)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 6 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons coconut oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Cook onions until golden and softened, about 8 minutes.
  4. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add ground meat and cook until browned, breaking it up.
  6. Stir in curry powder, turmeric, coriander, and cinnamon.
  7. Cook for 2 minutes until spices are fragrant.
  8. Add tomato paste, almonds, and raisins (if using).
  9. Pour in vinegar and stock, simmer for 10 minutes until thickened.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Transfer mixture to a greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
  12. Tuck bay leaves into the meat mixture.
  13. Beat eggs with cream and pour over meat mixture.
  14. The custard will set during baking.
  15. Bake for 30-35 minutes until custard is set and golden.
  16. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  17. Remove bay leaves before serving.
