Keto Ricotta Gnocchi with Brown Butter Sage

Light, fluffy ricotta gnocchi in a nutty brown butter sage sauce. An elegant Italian dish that's surprisingly keto-friendly.

#0139

⏰ Timing

Prep: 45 minutes
Cook: 15 minutes
Total: 1 hour

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta, drained
  • 1 large egg
  • 3/4 cup almond flour
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated, divided
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon salt, divided
  • 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 12 fresh sage leaves
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • Extra Parmesan for serving
  • Freshly ground black pepper

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Drain ricotta in a fine-mesh sieve for 30 minutes to remove excess moisture.
  2. In a large bowl, gently mix drained ricotta with egg until smooth.
  3. Add almond flour, 1/4 cup Parmesan, nutmeg, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and white pepper.
  4. Mix until just combined - don't overmix or gnocchi will be tough.
  5. With floured hands, roll mixture into small walnut-sized balls.
  6. Bring a large pot of salted water to a gentle simmer.
  7. Working in batches, drop gnocchi into simmering water.
  8. Cook for 2-3 minutes until they float to the surface.
  9. Remove with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  10. Meanwhile, heat butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
  11. Cook until butter turns golden brown and smells nutty, about 3-4 minutes.
  12. Add sage leaves and fry until crispy, about 1 minute.
  13. Add cooked gnocchi to brown butter and toss gently.
  14. Add lemon juice and remaining Parmesan.
  15. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper.
