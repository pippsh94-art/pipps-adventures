Keto Caprese Stuffed Chicken

Juicy chicken breasts stuffed with fresh mozzarella, tomatoes, and basil. Italian flavors in every bite.

#0112

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 45 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 2 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • Toothpicks for securing
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Create a pocket in each chicken breast by cutting horizontally about 3/4 through.
  3. Season chicken breasts inside and out with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  4. Stuff each pocket with mozzarella slices, tomato slices, and basil leaves.
  5. Secure with toothpicks.
  6. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  7. Sear stuffed chicken breasts for 3-4 minutes per side until golden.
  8. Transfer skillet to oven and bake for 18-22 minutes until internal temp reaches 165°F.
  9. Meanwhile, whisk together remaining olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and minced garlic.
  10. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes before removing toothpicks.
  11. Drizzle with balsamic mixture and garnish with fresh parsley.
  12. Serve with a side salad or roasted vegetables.
