Keto Loaded Cauliflower Bake

All the flavors of loaded baked potatoes but made with cauliflower. Topped with bacon, cheese, and green onions.

#0062

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 35 minutes
Total: 50 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
  • 8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 1/2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded, divided
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 4 green onions, chopped, divided
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Steam cauliflower florets for 12-15 minutes until very tender.
  3. Cook bacon until crispy, then crumble and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, mash steamed cauliflower with butter until smooth.
  5. Beat in cream cheese, sour cream, garlic, salt, and pepper until creamy.
  6. Stir in 1 cup cheddar cheese, half the bacon, and half the green onions.
  7. Transfer mixture to prepared baking dish and smooth the top.
  8. Top with remaining cheddar cheese, bacon, and green onions.
  9. Sprinkle with paprika for color.
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes until hot and bubbly.
  11. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
