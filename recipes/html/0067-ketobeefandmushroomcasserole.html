Keto Beef and Mushroom Casserole

Hearty casserole with ground beef, mushrooms, and a creamy cheese sauce. Comfort food that's family-friendly and filling.

#0067

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 30 minutes
Total: 50 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
    • \n
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
    • \n
  • 1 medium onion, diced
    • \n
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
    • \n
  • 1 cup beef broth
    • \n
  • 1 1/2 cups cheddar cheese, shredded, divided
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    2. \n
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    3. \n
  3. Cook ground beef, breaking it up, until browned and cooked through.
    4. \n
  4. Add diced onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
    5. \n
  5. Add mushrooms and cook for 5-6 minutes until golden.
    6. \n
  6. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
    7. \n
  7. Stir in cream cheese, beef broth, Worcestershire, thyme, salt, and pepper.
    8. \n
  8. Cook until cream cheese melts and sauce is smooth.
    9. \n
  9. Remove from heat and stir in sour cream and 1 cup cheddar cheese.
    10. \n
  10. Transfer mixture to prepared baking dish.
    11. \n
  11. Top with remaining cheddar cheese.
    12. \n
  12. Bake for 20-25 minutes until bubbly and golden.
    13. \n
  13. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
