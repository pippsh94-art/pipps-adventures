Keto Bacon-Wrapped Scallops

Succulent scallops wrapped in crispy bacon and pan-seared to perfection. An elegant appetizer or main course.

#0114

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 10 minutes
Total: 25 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 16 large sea scallops
  • 8 slices bacon, cut in half
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup white wine or chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Toothpicks for securing
  • Fresh parsley for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Remove scallops from refrigerator and pat completely dry with paper towels.
  2. Remove side muscles if still attached and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Wrap each scallop with a half slice of bacon and secure with a toothpick.
  4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  5. When oil is hot, add wrapped scallops without crowding the pan.
  6. Cook for 3-4 minutes per side until bacon is crispy and scallops are golden.
  7. Remove scallops to a plate and tent with foil.
  8. In the same pan, melt butter over medium heat.
  9. Add minced garlic and cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  10. Add wine or broth and lemon juice, simmer for 2 minutes.
  11. Stir in fresh thyme.
  12. Return scallops to pan briefly to warm through.
  13. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve with lemon wedges.
