Keto Mediterranean Chicken Bowls

Fresh Mediterranean flavors with grilled chicken, olives, feta, and a tangy lemon dressing. Perfect for meal prep.

#0076

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 15 minutes
Total: 35 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
    • \n
  • 4 cups mixed greens
    • \n
  • 1 cucumber, diced
    • \n
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
    • \n
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup kalamata olives
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese, crumbled
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
    • \n
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
    • \n
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon oregano
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • \n
  • Fresh herbs for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt, pepper, and half the oregano.
    2. \n
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a grill pan or skillet over medium-high heat.
    3. \n
  3. Cook chicken for 6-7 minutes per side until golden and cooked through.
    4. \n
  4. Let chicken rest for 5 minutes, then slice into strips.
    5. \n
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together remaining olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
    6. \n
  6. Divide mixed greens among 4 bowls.
    7. \n
  7. Top each bowl with sliced chicken, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olives, and feta cheese.
    8. \n
  8. Drizzle with lemon dressing.
    9. \n
  9. Garnish with fresh herbs.
    10. \n
  10. Serve immediately or store components separately for meal prep.
