Keto Pork Tenderloin with Herb Crust

Juicy pork tenderloin with a flavorful herb and almond flour crust. An impressive main dish that's surprisingly simple.

#0098

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 40 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pork tenderloins (1 pound each)
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh sage, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Pat pork tenderloins dry and season all over with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear tenderloins for 2-3 minutes per side until golden brown on all sides.
  5. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  6. In a bowl, mix almond flour, parsley, rosemary, sage, garlic, and melted butter.
  7. Brush each tenderloin with Dijon mustard.
  8. Press herb mixture firmly onto the mustard-coated pork.
  9. Transfer skillet to oven and roast for 15-20 minutes until internal temp reaches 145°F.
  10. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  11. Slice into medallions and serve with roasted vegetables.
  12. Drizzle any pan juices over the sliced pork.
