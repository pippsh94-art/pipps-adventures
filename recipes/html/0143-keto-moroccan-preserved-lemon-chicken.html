Keto Moroccan Preserved Lemon Chicken

Aromatic chicken with preserved lemons, olives, and warm Moroccan spices. An exotic journey to North African flavors.

#0143

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 45 minutes
Total: 1 hour 5 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 chicken thighs, bone-in and skin-on
  • 4 chicken drumsticks
  • 2 preserved lemons, quartered (or lemon zest)
  • 1 cup green olives, pitted
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Season chicken pieces with salt and pepper.
  3. Mix cinnamon, cumin, turmeric, and ground ginger in a small bowl.
  4. Rub spice mixture all over chicken pieces.
  5. Heat olive oil in a large oven-safe Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  6. Brown chicken pieces skin-side down for 5-6 minutes until golden.
  7. Flip and brown other side for 3-4 minutes. Remove chicken.
  8. Add onion to the same pot and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  9. Add garlic and fresh ginger, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  10. Return chicken to pot and add preserved lemons and olives.
  11. Pour in chicken broth around the chicken.
  12. Cover and transfer to oven.
  13. Braise for 30-35 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  14. Garnish with fresh cilantro and parsley before serving.
