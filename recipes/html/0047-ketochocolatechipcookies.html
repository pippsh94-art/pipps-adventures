Keto Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft, chewy keto chocolate chip cookies made with almond flour. Sweet satisfaction without the sugar crash.

#0047

⏰ Timing

Prep: 10 minutes
Cook: 12 minutes
Total: 22 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup erythritol, granulated
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted
    • \n
  • 1 large egg
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup sugar-free chocolate chips
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons heavy cream

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
    2. \n
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together almond flour, erythritol, baking soda, and salt.
    3. \n
  3. In another bowl, mix melted butter, egg, vanilla, and heavy cream.
    4. \n
  4. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and stir until combined.
    5. \n
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
    6. \n
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto prepared baking sheet, spacing 2 inches apart.
    7. \n
  7. Gently flatten each cookie with the back of a spoon.
    8. \n
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until edges are lightly golden.
    9. \n
  9. Let cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to wire rack.
    10. \n
  10. Store in airtight container for up to one week.
