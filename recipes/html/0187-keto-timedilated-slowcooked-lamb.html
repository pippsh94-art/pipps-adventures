Keto Time-Dilated Slow-Cooked Lamb

Temporal cooking chamber that compresses 24 hours of cooking into 2 hours using time manipulation technology.

#0187

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 2 hours (24 hours subjective time)
Total: 2 hours 30 minutes

  • Temporal cooking chamber

  3. Set temporal acceleration ratio to 12:1 (24 hours in 2 hours).

  15. Rest lamb for 15 minutes to stabilize temporal proteins.
  16. Carve and serve with vegetables that have experienced extended cooking.
  17. The result is impossible: 24-hour flavor in 2-hour time.
