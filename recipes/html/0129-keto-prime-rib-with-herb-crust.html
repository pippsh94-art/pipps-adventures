Keto Prime Rib with Herb Crust

Standing rib roast with a flavorful herb crust. The ultimate centerpiece for special occasions and holidays.

#0129

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 2 hours 30 minutes
Total: 2 hours 50 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 standing rib roast (6-7 pounds, 3-4 ribs)
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons fresh thyme, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh sage, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons coarse salt
  • 1 tablespoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 4 carrots, cut into chunks
  • 4 celery stalks, cut into chunks
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1/4 cup red wine (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Remove roast from refrigerator 2 hours before cooking to bring to room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 450°F.
  3. Mix olive oil, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Pat roast dry and brush all over with Dijon mustard.
  5. Rub herb mixture evenly over the entire roast.
  6. Place vegetables in bottom of roasting pan and set roast on top, bone-side down.
  7. Roast at 450°F for 20 minutes to develop crust.
  8. Reduce temperature to 325°F.
  9. Continue roasting for 15-20 minutes per pound for medium-rare (internal temp 130°F).
  10. Add beef broth and wine to pan during cooking to prevent burning.
  11. Remove when internal temperature reaches 125°F for medium-rare.
  12. Tent with foil and let rest for 20 minutes before carving.
  13. Temperature will rise to 130-135°F while resting.
  14. Carve between bones and serve with pan drippings.
