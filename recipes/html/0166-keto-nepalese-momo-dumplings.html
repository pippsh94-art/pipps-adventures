Keto Nepalese Momo Dumplings

Himalayan steamed dumplings with spiced meat filling. Mountain cuisine that bridges Tibet and India with incredible flavor.

#0166

⏰ Timing

Prep: 1 hour
Cook: 20 minutes
Total: 1 hour 20 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups almond flour
  • 1/4 cup coconut flour
  • 2 tablespoons psyllium husk
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1.5 pounds ground yak (or lamb)
  • 1 large onion, finely minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 2 teaspoons cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon asafoetida
  • 2 tablespoons fresh cilantro, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Tomato-based dipping sauce

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. For wrapper dough: Mix almond flour, coconut flour, psyllium husk, and salt.
  2. Add hot water and olive oil, mix until smooth dough forms.
  3. Knead for 5 minutes until elastic. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  4. For filling: Combine ground meat with onion, garlic, ginger, and all spices.
  5. Mix in cilantro and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Let filling rest for 20 minutes to develop flavors.
  7. Divide dough into 20-24 small portions.
  8. Roll each portion into thin circles (about 3 inches).
  9. Place 1 tablespoon filling in center of each wrapper.
  10. Gather edges and pleat to form dumpling pouches.
  11. Seal edges tightly to prevent opening during steaming.
  12. Steam dumplings in bamboo steamer for 15-20 minutes.
  13. Dumplings should be translucent and filling cooked through.
  14. Serve hot with spicy tomato dipping sauce.
  15. Traditional accompaniment includes fermented radish pickle.
