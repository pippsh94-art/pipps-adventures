Keto Vanilla Panna Cotta

Silky smooth Italian dessert with rich vanilla flavor. Elegant, make-ahead, and perfectly portioned for special occasions.

#0090

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 5 minutes
Total: 4 hours 20 minutes (including chilling)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup erythritol, granulated
    • \n
  • 1 packet unflavored gelatin
    • \n
  • 3 tablespoons warm water
    • \n
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • Fresh berries for garnish
    • \n
  • Sugar-free whipped cream (optional)
    • \n
  • Mint leaves for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Sprinkle gelatin over warm water in a small bowl and let bloom for 5 minutes.
    2. \n
  2. In a saucepan, heat 1 cup heavy cream with erythritol over medium heat.
    3. \n
  3. Stir until erythritol dissolves completely but don't let it boil.
    4. \n
  4. Remove from heat and whisk in the bloomed gelatin until dissolved.
    5. \n
  5. Stir in remaining cold cream, vanilla extract, and salt.
    6. \n
  6. Strain mixture through a fine-mesh sieve to remove any lumps.
    7. \n
  7. Divide mixture evenly among 6 serving glasses or ramekins.
    8. \n
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight until set.
    9. \n
  9. Before serving, top with fresh berries and mint leaves.
    10. \n
  10. Add a dollop of sugar-free whipped cream if desired.
    11. \n
  11. Serve chilled directly from the glasses.
