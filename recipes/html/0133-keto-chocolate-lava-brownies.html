Keto Chocolate Lava Brownies

Fudgy brownies with molten chocolate centers. Decadent, rich, and the ultimate keto chocolate dessert.

#0133

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 45 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 6 ounces sugar-free dark chocolate, chopped
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup erythritol, granulated
  • 1/3 cup almond flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 ounces sugar-free chocolate chunks
  • Butter for greasing
  • Sugar-free whipped cream for serving
  • Fresh berries for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease an 8x8 inch baking pan.
  2. Melt chocolate and butter in a double boiler until smooth. Let cool slightly.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk eggs and erythritol until light and fluffy, about 3 minutes.
  4. Stir melted chocolate mixture and vanilla into egg mixture.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk together almond flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
  6. Fold dry ingredients into chocolate mixture until just combined.
  7. Fold in chocolate chunks, reserving some for topping.
  8. Pour batter into prepared pan and smooth top.
  9. Sprinkle remaining chocolate chunks on top.
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes until edges are set but center still jiggles slightly.
  11. The center should be fudgy and molten.
  12. Let cool for 10 minutes before cutting into squares.
  13. Serve warm with whipped cream and fresh berries.
  14. Store covered for up to 3 days, reheat briefly to restore gooey center.
