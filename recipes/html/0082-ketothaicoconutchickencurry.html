Keto Thai Coconut Chicken Curry

Authentic Thai flavors with tender chicken in a rich coconut curry sauce. Aromatic, creamy, and perfectly spiced.

#0082

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 40 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk, full-fat
  • 3 tablespoons red curry paste
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 Thai eggplant, cubed (or regular eggplant)
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon erythritol
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 Thai chilies, sliced (optional)
  • Lime wedges for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Heat coconut oil in a large pan over medium-high heat.
  2. Add chicken chunks and cook for 5-6 minutes until lightly browned.
  3. Remove chicken and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, add curry paste and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add thick part of coconut milk (from top of can) and stir with curry paste.
  6. Add remaining coconut milk, onion, garlic, and ginger.
  7. Bring to a simmer and add bell pepper and eggplant.
  8. Return chicken to pan and simmer for 15 minutes until tender.
  9. Stir in fish sauce, lime juice, and erythritol.
  10. Add Thai chilies if using and fresh basil leaves.
  11. Simmer for 2 more minutes until basil wilts.
  12. Serve hot with lime wedges and over cauliflower rice.
