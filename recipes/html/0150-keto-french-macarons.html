Keto French Macarons

Delicate almond cookies with ganache filling. The ultimate French pastry technique mastered for keto - a true culinary triumph.

#0150

⏰ Timing

Prep: 2 hours
Cook: 20 minutes
Total: 6 hours (including resting)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 100g almond flour, super fine
  • 200g powdered erythritol, sifted
  • 75g egg whites, aged overnight
  • 25g granulated erythritol
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 150ml heavy cream
  • 150g sugar-free dark chocolate, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Natural flavor extracts (vanilla, raspberry, etc.)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Sift almond flour and powdered erythritol together three times. Set aside.
  2. Whip egg whites to soft peaks, gradually add granulated erythritol.
  3. Whip to stiff, glossy peaks. Add food coloring and vanilla if using.
  4. Add 1/3 of almond flour mixture to meringue. Fold gently.
  5. Continue adding flour mixture in two more additions, folding until smooth.
  6. The batter should flow like thick ribbon when lifted.
  7. Transfer to piping bag and pipe 1.5-inch circles on parchment-lined baking sheets.
  8. Tap sheets firmly to release air bubbles.
  9. Let sit at room temperature for 30-60 minutes until tops are no longer tacky.
  10. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  11. Bake for 15-20 minutes until feet have formed and shells don't move when touched.
  12. Cool completely before removing from parchment.
  13. For ganache: Heat cream and pour over chocolate. Whisk until smooth.
  14. Cool until spreadable, then sandwich shells together.
  15. Age assembled macarons in refrigerator for 24 hours before serving.
