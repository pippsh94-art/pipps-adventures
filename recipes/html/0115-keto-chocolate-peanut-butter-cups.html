Keto Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups

Homemade chocolate peanut butter cups with no sugar added. Rich, creamy, and perfectly portioned for keto dessert cravings.

#0115

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 0 minutes
Total: 2 hours 20 minutes (including chilling)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 8 ounces sugar-free dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup powdered erythritol
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 12 mini muffin cup liners

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Line a 12-cup mini muffin tin with paper liners.
  2. Melt half the chocolate (4 oz) in a double boiler or microwave in 30-second intervals.
  3. Spoon about 1 tablespoon melted chocolate into each liner, spreading to cover bottom.
  4. Place in refrigerator for 15 minutes to set.
  5. Meanwhile, mix peanut butter, powdered erythritol, 1 tablespoon coconut oil, vanilla, and salt until smooth.
  6. Remove muffin tin from refrigerator.
  7. Spoon about 1 teaspoon peanut butter mixture into each cup.
  8. Gently spread, leaving a small border around edges.
  9. Melt remaining chocolate with remaining coconut oil.
  10. Spoon melted chocolate over peanut butter layer, covering completely.
  11. Tap tin gently to smooth tops.
  12. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours until firm.
  13. Store in refrigerator for up to 1 week.
