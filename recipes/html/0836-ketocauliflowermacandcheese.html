Keto Cauliflower Mac and Cheese

All the comfort of traditional mac and cheese but made with cauliflower florets and a rich, creamy cheese sauce. Kid-approved!

#0836

⏰ Timing

Prep: 10 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 35 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 large head cauliflower, cut into florets
    • \n
  • 4 tablespoons butter
    • \n
  • 3 ounces cream cheese
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • \n
  • 1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, grated
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, grated
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard powder
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons chopped chives for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
    2. \n
  2. Steam cauliflower florets for 8-10 minutes until tender. Drain well.
    3. \n
  3. In a large saucepan, melt butter over medium heat.
    4. \n
  4. Add cream cheese and heavy cream, whisking until smooth.
    5. \n
  5. Gradually whisk in cheddar and mozzarella cheese until melted and smooth.
    6. \n
  6. Season with garlic powder, mustard powder, salt, and pepper.
    7. \n
  7. Add steamed cauliflower to the cheese sauce and gently stir to coat.
    8. \n
  8. Transfer to prepared baking dish and bake for 15 minutes until bubbly.
    9. \n
  9. Garnish with chopped chives before serving.
