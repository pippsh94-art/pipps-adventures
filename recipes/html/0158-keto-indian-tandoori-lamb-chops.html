Keto Indian Tandoori Lamb Chops

Yogurt-marinated lamb chops with aromatic spices, grilled to perfection. Bold Indian flavors that transport you to Delhi.

#0158

⏰ Timing

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 15 minutes
Total: 4 hours 45 minutes (including marinating)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 8 lamb chops, frenched
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Lemon wedges for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Make shallow cuts in the lamb chops to help marinade penetrate.
  2. In a large bowl, combine yogurt, garlic, ginger, lemon juice, and all spices.
  3. Add lamb chops and coat thoroughly with marinade.
  4. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  5. Remove lamb from refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking.
  6. Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  7. Remove excess marinade from lamb chops.
  8. Brush grill grates with oil to prevent sticking.
  9. Grill lamb chops for 4-5 minutes per side for medium-rare.
  10. Internal temperature should reach 135°F.
  11. The marinade will create a beautiful charred exterior.
  12. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  13. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lemon wedges.
  14. Excellent with cucumber raita and grilled vegetables.
