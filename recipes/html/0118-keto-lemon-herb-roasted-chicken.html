Keto Lemon Herb Roasted Chicken

Whole roasted chicken with bright lemon and fresh herbs. Crispy skin, juicy meat, and perfect for Sunday dinner.

#0118

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 1 hour 15 minutes
Total: 1 hour 30 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (4-5 pounds)
  • 2 lemons, one halved and one sliced
  • 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme
  • 1 tablespoon fresh sage, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 onion, quartered
  • 4 carrots, cut into chunks
  • Fresh herbs for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  2. Pat chicken dry inside and out with paper towels.
  3. Mix softened butter with minced garlic and half the fresh herbs.
  4. Carefully loosen skin over breast and thighs, spread herb butter under skin.
  5. Stuff cavity with lemon halves, remaining herbs, and some onion quarters.
  6. Truss chicken with kitchen twine.
  7. Rub skin with olive oil and season generously with salt and pepper.
  8. Place chicken breast-side up on a rack in a roasting pan.
  9. Surround with remaining onion quarters, carrots, and lemon slices.
  10. Roast for 60-75 minutes until internal temp reaches 165°F in thickest part of thigh.
  11. Let rest for 15 minutes before carving.
  12. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve with the roasted vegetables.
  13. Save the pan drippings for gravy if desired.
