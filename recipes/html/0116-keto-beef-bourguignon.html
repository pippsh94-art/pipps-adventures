Keto Beef Bourguignon

Classic French beef stew with red wine, mushrooms, and herbs. Rich, elegant, and perfect for special occasions.

#0116

⏰ Timing

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 2 hours 30 minutes
Total: 3 hours

🛒 Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef chuck roast, cut into 2-inch cubes
  • 6 slices bacon, chopped
  • 1 pound cremini mushrooms, halved
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 carrots, cut into chunks
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 bottle (750ml) dry red wine
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons almond flour
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Season beef cubes with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook bacon in a large Dutch oven until crispy. Remove and set aside.
  4. Brown beef cubes in bacon fat in batches, about 4-5 minutes per side.
  5. Remove beef and set aside with bacon.
  6. Add mushrooms to pot and cook until browned, about 8 minutes.
  7. Remove mushrooms and set aside.
  8. Add onion and carrots, cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  9. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook for 1 minute.
  10. Pour in red wine, scraping up browned bits.
  11. Return beef and bacon to pot.
  12. Add beef broth, bay leaves, and thyme.
  13. Bring to a simmer, then cover and transfer to oven.
  14. Cook for 2 hours until beef is tender.
  15. Return mushrooms to pot and cook 30 minutes more.
  16. If desired, thicken with almond flour mixed with a little broth.
  17. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
