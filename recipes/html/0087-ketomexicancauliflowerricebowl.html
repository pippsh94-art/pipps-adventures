Keto Mexican Cauliflower Rice Bowl

Flavorful Mexican-spiced cauliflower rice topped with avocado, cheese, and fresh salsa. A complete meal in a bowl.

#0087

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 15 minutes
Total: 30 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1 large head cauliflower, riced
    • \n
  • 1 pound ground beef
    • \n
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
    • \n
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
    • \n
  • 1 avocado, diced
    • \n
  • 1 cup Mexican cheese blend, shredded
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
    • \n
  • 1 jalapeño, minced (optional)
    • \n
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    • \n
  • Lime wedges for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Rice cauliflower in a food processor until it resembles rice grains.
    2. \n
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    3. \n
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks.
    4. \n
  4. Add taco seasoning and diced tomatoes. Simmer for 5 minutes.
    5. \n
  5. In another skillet, heat remaining oil over medium-high heat.
    6. \n
  6. Add cauliflower rice and cook for 5-7 minutes until tender.
    7. \n
  7. Season cauliflower rice with salt and pepper.
    8. \n
  8. Divide cauliflower rice among serving bowls.
    9. \n
  9. Top with seasoned ground beef.
    10. \n
  10. Add diced avocado, cheese, red onion, and cilantro.
    11. \n
  11. Drizzle with lime juice and serve with sour cream.
    12. \n
  12. Garnish with jalapeño if desired and serve with lime wedges.
