Keto Cheesy Broccoli Casserole

Classic broccoli casserole made keto-friendly with a rich cheese sauce and crunchy almond topping. The perfect side dish.

#0075

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 25 minutes
Total: 40 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pounds fresh broccoli florets
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 1/2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded, divided
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and grease a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  2. Steam broccoli florets for 5-6 minutes until crisp-tender. Drain well.
  3. In a large bowl, mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, eggs, garlic, onion powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Stir in 1 cup cheddar cheese and all the mozzarella cheese.
  5. Add steamed broccoli and gently fold to coat with cheese mixture.
  6. Transfer to prepared baking dish and smooth the top.
  7. Sprinkle remaining cheddar cheese over the top.
  8. In a small bowl, toss sliced almonds with melted butter.
  9. Sprinkle buttered almonds over the cheese.
  10. Bake for 20-25 minutes until bubbly and golden on top.
  11. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving.
