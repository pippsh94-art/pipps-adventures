Keto Australian Barramundi with Macadamia Crust

Pan-seared barramundi with native macadamia nut crust. Australian coastal cuisine at its finest with tropical flavors.

#0159

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 15 minutes
Total: 35 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 barramundi fillets (6 ounces each)
  • 1 cup macadamia nuts, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup almond flour
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and white pepper to taste
  • Australian bush tomatoes for garnish (or sun-dried)
  • Microgreens for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
  2. Season barramundi fillets with salt and white pepper.
  3. In a food processor, pulse macadamia nuts until roughly chopped (don't over-process).
  4. Mix chopped macadamias with almond flour, parsley, lemon zest, and garlic.
  5. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons olive oil and mix until just combined.
  6. Heat remaining olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  7. Sear barramundi fillets skin-side down for 3-4 minutes until crispy.
  8. Flip fillets and top each with macadamia mixture, pressing gently.
  9. Transfer skillet to oven and bake for 8-10 minutes until fish flakes easily.
  10. Remove from oven and let rest for 2 minutes.
  11. In a small pan, melt butter with lemon juice until foaming.
  12. Drizzle lemon butter over fish.
  13. Garnish with bush tomatoes and microgreens.
  14. Serve immediately with grilled asparagus or a light salad.
