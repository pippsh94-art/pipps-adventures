Keto Duck Confit with Orange Glaze

Slow-cooked duck legs in their own fat with a bright orange glaze. An elegant French technique that creates incredibly tender, flavorful meat.

#0121

⏰ Timing

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 3 hours
Total: 3 hours 30 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 duck legs
  • 1/4 cup coarse sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 2 cups duck fat (or olive oil)
  • 1/4 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons orange zest
  • 2 tablespoons erythritol
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary
  • Salt and pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Season duck legs with coarse salt, thyme, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  2. Cure in refrigerator for 2-24 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 225°F.
  4. Rinse duck legs and pat completely dry.
  5. Place in a deep baking dish and cover with duck fat.
  6. Cook for 2.5-3 hours until meat easily pulls from bone.
  7. Meanwhile, make glaze by simmering orange juice, zest, erythritol, and mustard until reduced by half.
  8. Remove duck from fat and pat dry.
  9. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  10. Crisp duck skin-side down for 3-4 minutes until golden.
  11. Brush with orange glaze and garnish with fresh rosemary.
  12. Serve with roasted vegetables or green salad.
  13. Save the duck fat for roasting vegetables!
