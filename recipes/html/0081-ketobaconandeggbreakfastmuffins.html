Keto Bacon and Egg Breakfast Muffins

Fluffy, portable breakfast muffins loaded with bacon and eggs. Perfect for busy mornings and meal prep.

#0081

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 20 minutes
Total: 35 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
    • \n
  • 8 slices bacon, cooked and crumbled
    • \n
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
    • \n
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup chives, chopped
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper, diced
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
    • \n
  • Cooking spray

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and spray a 12-cup muffin tin with cooking spray.
    2. \n
  2. Cook bacon until crispy, then crumble and set aside.
    3. \n
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, heavy cream, salt, and pepper.
    4. \n
  4. Stir in melted butter, cheese, chives, and bell pepper.
    5. \n
  5. Divide crumbled bacon evenly among muffin cups.
    6. \n
  6. Pour egg mixture over bacon, filling each cup about 3/4 full.
    7. \n
  7. Sprinkle tops with paprika.
    8. \n
  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes until eggs are set and lightly golden.
    9. \n
  9. Let cool in pan for 5 minutes before removing.
    10. \n
  10. Serve warm or store in refrigerator for up to 5 days.
