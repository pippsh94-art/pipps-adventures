Keto Jamaican Jerk Chicken

Fiery Caribbean spice blend with scotch bonnet peppers. Island heat that brings the beaches of Jamaica to your plate.

#0169

⏰ Timing

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 45 minutes
Total: 4 hours 15 minutes (including marinating)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 pounds chicken pieces (thighs and legs)
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers, seeded and minced
  • 6 green onions, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-inch piece ginger, minced
  • 1/4 cup lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
  • 2 tablespoons coconut aminos
  • 2 teaspoons allspice
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon cloves
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Lime wedges for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. In a food processor, combine scotch bonnets, green onions, garlic, ginger, lime juice, and coconut oil.
  2. Pulse until you have a rough paste.
  3. Add coconut aminos, allspice, thyme, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, salt, and pepper.
  4. Process until smooth - this is your jerk marinade.
  5. Score chicken skin in a crosshatch pattern to help marinade penetrate.
  6. Rub jerk marinade all over chicken pieces, getting into scores.
  7. Marinate in refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  8. Preheat grill to medium heat with both direct and indirect zones.
  9. Remove chicken from marinade and let come to room temperature.
  10. Grill chicken over direct heat for 5-6 minutes per side to char.
  11. Move to indirect heat and continue cooking for 25-30 minutes.
  12. Internal temperature should reach 165°F.
  13. The skin should be deeply charred and crispy.
  14. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  15. Serve with lime wedges and traditional rice and peas (use cauliflower).
