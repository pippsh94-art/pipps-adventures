Keto Turkish Lamb Kebabs with Tzatziki

Spiced ground lamb kebabs with cooling tzatziki sauce. Mediterranean flavors that transport you to the Turkish coast.

#0146

⏰ Timing

Prep: 30 minutes
Cook: 15 minutes
Total: 45 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground lamb
  • 1 large onion, finely minced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh mint, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cucumber, grated and drained
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill
  • Salt to taste

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. For tzatziki: Grate cucumber and squeeze in clean kitchen towel to remove excess water.
  2. Mix cucumber with yogurt, minced garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, dill, and salt.
  3. Refrigerate while preparing kebabs.
  4. In a large bowl, combine lamb, onion, garlic, parsley, mint, cumin, paprika, cinnamon, salt, and pepper.
  5. Mix gently with hands until just combined - don't overmix.
  6. Form mixture around metal skewers into sausage shapes.
  7. Refrigerate for 20 minutes to help kebabs hold their shape.
  8. Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  9. Brush kebabs lightly with olive oil.
  10. Grill for 12-15 minutes, turning every 3-4 minutes until cooked through.
  11. Internal temperature should reach 160°F.
  12. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  13. Serve hot with tzatziki sauce and fresh herbs.
  14. Great with grilled vegetables or a Greek salad.
