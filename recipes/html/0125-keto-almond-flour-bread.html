Keto Almond Flour Bread

Soft, fluffy bread made with almond flour. Perfect for sandwiches, toast, or just eating fresh with butter.

#0125

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 45 minutes
Total: 1 hour

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cups super fine almond flour
  • 1/4 cup psyllium husk powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 6 large eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds (optional)
  • Butter for greasing

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together almond flour, psyllium husk, baking powder, xanthan gum, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk egg yolks with olive oil, almond milk, and vinegar.
  4. In a clean bowl, whip egg whites until stiff peaks form.
  5. Add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
  6. Gently fold in 1/3 of the whipped egg whites to lighten mixture.
  7. Fold in remaining egg whites in two additions, being careful not to deflate.
  8. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan and smooth top.
  9. Sprinkle with sesame seeds if using.
  10. Bake for 40-45 minutes until golden brown and a toothpick comes out clean.
  11. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto wire rack.
  12. Let cool completely before slicing for best texture.
  13. Store covered at room temperature for 3 days or freeze for up to 3 months.
