Keto Stuffed Bell Peppers

Colorful bell peppers stuffed with a hearty mixture of ground beef, cauliflower rice, and melted cheese. A complete meal in one dish.

#0839

⏰ Timing

Prep: 20 minutes
Cook: 30 minutes
Total: 50 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 4 large bell peppers, tops cut and seeded
    • \n
  • 1 pound ground beef
    • \n
  • 1 small onion, diced
    • \n
  • 2 cups cauliflower rice
    • \n
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes, drained
    • \n
  • 1 cup shredded Mexican cheese blend, divided
    • \n
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
    2. \n
  2. Cut tops off peppers and remove seeds and membranes. If needed, trim bottom slightly so peppers stand upright.
    3. \n
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
    4. \n
  4. Add onion and cook for 5 minutes until softened.
    5. \n
  5. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks.
    6. \n
  6. Add garlic, cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Cook 1 minute.
    7. \n
  7. Stir in cauliflower rice and diced tomatoes. Cook for 5 minutes.
    8. \n
  8. Remove from heat and stir in half the cheese.
    9. \n
  9. Stuff each pepper with the beef mixture and place in a baking dish.
    10. \n
  10. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    11. \n
  11. Remove foil, top with remaining cheese, and bake 5 more minutes until cheese melts.
