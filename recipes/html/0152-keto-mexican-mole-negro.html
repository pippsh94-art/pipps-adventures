Keto Mexican Mole Negro

Complex Mexican sauce with chocolate and dozens of spices. The ultimate expression of Mexican culinary artistry in keto form.

#0152

⏰ Timing

Prep: 2 hours
Cook: 3 hours
Total: 5 hours

🛒 Ingredients

  • 6 dried chiles (ancho, mulato, chipotle)
  • 4 tomatoes, charred
  • 1 onion, charred
  • 6 cloves garlic, charred
  • 2 ounces sugar-free dark chocolate
  • 1/4 cup almonds, toasted
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon pumpkin seeds
  • 2 corn tortillas, toasted (keto version)
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon anise seeds
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons lard or coconut oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chicken pieces for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Toast chiles in a dry pan until fragrant but not burnt. Remove seeds.
  2. Soak chiles in hot water for 30 minutes until soft.
  3. Char tomatoes, onion, and garlic over open flame until blackened.
  4. Toast almonds, sesame seeds, pumpkin seeds, and spices separately.
  5. In a blender, puree soaked chiles with their soaking liquid until smooth.
  6. Strain chile mixture through fine-mesh sieve.
  7. Blend charred vegetables with some broth until smooth.
  8. Blend nuts, seeds, tortillas, and spices with remaining broth.
  9. Heat lard in a large pot over medium heat.
  10. Fry chile puree for 10 minutes, stirring constantly.
  11. Add vegetable puree and cook 10 minutes more.
  12. Add nut mixture and chocolate, simmer 45 minutes.
  13. Season with salt and adjust consistency with broth.
  14. Mole should coat the back of a spoon.
  15. Serve over grilled chicken or turkey.
