Keto Coconut Curry Shrimp

Creamy coconut curry with plump shrimp and aromatic spices. Exotic flavors that transport you to the tropics.

#0066

⏰ Timing

Prep: 15 minutes
Cook: 15 minutes
Total: 30 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined
    • \n
  • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk, full-fat
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons red curry paste
    • \n
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
    • \n
  • 1 small onion, sliced
    • \n
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons coconut oil
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
    • \n
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon erythritol
    • \n
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
    • \n
  • Lime wedges for serving

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Pat shrimp dry and season with salt.
    2. \n
  2. Heat coconut oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
    3. \n
  3. Add shrimp and cook for 2-3 minutes per side until pink. Remove and set aside.
    4. \n
  4. In the same skillet, add onion and bell pepper.
    5. \n
  5. Cook for 5 minutes until softened.
    6. \n
  6. Add garlic, ginger, and curry paste. Cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
    7. \n
  7. Pour in coconut milk, fish sauce, lime juice, and erythritol.
    8. \n
  8. Simmer for 5 minutes until sauce thickens slightly.
    9. \n
  9. Return shrimp to the skillet and toss to coat.
    10. \n
  10. Cook for 2-3 minutes until heated through.
    11. \n
  11. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges.
    12. \n
  12. Serve over cauliflower rice if desired.
