Keto Edible Hologram Beef Tartare

Futuristic dining experience with projected flavors and augmented reality garnish. The intersection of food and technology.

#0183

⏰ Timing

Prep: 2 hours
Cook: 0 minutes
Total: 2 hours

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 pounds premium beef tenderloin
  • Holographic film (food-safe)
  • LED projection system
  • Edible glitter
  • Flavor-changing powder
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 1 shallot, minced
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Microprocessor-controlled serving plates
  • AR glasses for full experience

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Hand-cut beef tenderloin into perfect 1/4-inch dice with sharp knife.
  2. Keep beef extremely cold throughout preparation.
  3. Set up LED projection system to create holographic effects.
  4. Program serving plates with synchronized light patterns.
  5. Mix minced shallot, capers, mustard, and olive oil for base.
  6. Gently fold diced beef with base mixture.
  7. Form beef mixture into perfect quenelle shapes.
  8. Place each portion on programmed serving plate.
  9. Top with fresh egg yolk for traditional presentation.
  10. Activate holographic system to project changing colors around the beef.
  11. Sprinkle with edible glitter that reacts to LED lights.
  12. Add flavor-changing powder that responds to temperature.
  13. Guests wear AR glasses to see additional virtual garnishes.
  14. The presentation changes color and virtual elements as it's consumed.
  15. This represents the future of interactive dining experiences.
