Keto Lobster Bisque

Rich, creamy lobster bisque with chunks of tender lobster meat. An elegant soup perfect for special occasions.

#0124

⏰ Timing

Prep: 25 minutes
Cook: 45 minutes
Total: 1 hour 10 minutes

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 cooked lobster tails, meat removed and shells reserved
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup dry sherry
  • 4 cups seafood stock
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon fresh tarragon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Fresh chives for garnish

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Remove lobster meat from shells and chop into chunks. Reserve shells.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoons butter in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Add lobster shells and cook for 5 minutes to develop flavor.
  4. Add onion, celery, and carrot. Cook for 8 minutes until softened.
  5. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  6. Pour in sherry and cook for 2 minutes to reduce alcohol.
  7. Add seafood stock and bring to a simmer.
  8. Simmer for 20 minutes to extract flavors from shells.
  9. Strain broth through fine-mesh sieve, pressing solids. Discard solids.
  10. Return strained broth to pot and bring to a gentle simmer.
  11. Whisk in heavy cream and cream cheese until smooth.
  12. Add tarragon, paprika, salt, and cayenne.
  13. Stir in lobster meat and remaining butter.
  14. Heat through for 3-4 minutes. Don't boil or cream may curdle.
  15. Garnish with fresh chives and serve immediately.
