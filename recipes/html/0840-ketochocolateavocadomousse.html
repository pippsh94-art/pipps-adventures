Keto Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Rich and creamy chocolate mousse made with avocados for a healthy twist. No one will guess the secret ingredient!

#0840

⏰ Timing

Prep: 10 minutes
Cook: 0 minutes
Total: 10 minutes (plus 2 hours chilling)

🛒 Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe avocados
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
    • \n
  • 1/4 cup powdered erythritol
    • \n
  • 3 tablespoons heavy cream
    • \n
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter
    • \n
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • \n
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • \n
  • 2 oz sugar-free dark chocolate, melted
    • \n
  • Whipped cream for serving
    • \n
  • Berries for garnish (optional)

👩‍🍳 Instructions

  1. Cut avocados in half, remove pits, and scoop flesh into a food processor.
    2. \n
  2. Add cocoa powder, erythritol, heavy cream, almond butter, vanilla, and salt.
    3. \n
  3. Process until completely smooth and creamy, scraping down sides as needed.
    4. \n
  4. Add melted chocolate and process until fully incorporated.
    5. \n
  5. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.
    6. \n
  6. Divide mousse among 4 serving glasses or bowls.
    7. \n
  7. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours until set.
    8. \n
  8. Serve topped with whipped cream and berries if desired.
