Reverse Stuffed Shells

Original cookbook page 664

Ingredients

  • Jumbo shells
  • Ricotta
  • Shredded mozzarella- divided
  • Grated parmesan
  • e Egg yolk
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook jumbo shells according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix ricotta, half the shredded mozzarella, grated parmesan, egg yolk, Italian seasoning, and salt.
  3. Spread marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  4. Fill each shell with the cheese mixture and place in the dish.
  5. Top with remaining mozzarella and more marinara sauce.
  6. Bake at 375°F for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.
