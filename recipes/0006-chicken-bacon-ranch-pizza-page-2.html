Chicken Bacon Ranch Pizza (Page 2)

Original cookbook page 6

Instructions page for the low-carb chicken bacon ranch pizza with ground chicken crust and homemade ranch topping.

Cook: 30-40 minutes

Ingredients

  • Ground chicken
  • Mozzarella cheese (for crust and topping)
  • Ranch dressing ingredients
  • Bacon
  • Parchment paper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together ground chicken and mozzarella. Form into a ball.
  3. Place parchment paper on flat surface and place your crust.
  4. Place another sheet of parchment on top and roll out into round circle, about 1/2 inch thick.
  5. Place crust on parchment on baking tray and bake 20-25 minutes, until brown and loses moisture.
  6. While crust cooks, make ranch by whisking all ingredients together, and brown the bacon.
  7. Remove crust from oven and place 2 tbsp ranch in center, spreading with spoon to create circle, leaving 1/2-3/4 inch for crust.
  8. Place 1 cup mozzarella cheese on top of ranch, then add bacon.
  9. Bake another 10-15 minutes until cheese melted.
  10. Broil 1-2 minutes until crust browned and cheese golden brown.
  11. Remove from oven and slice.
