Keto BLT Chaffle

Original cookbook page 143

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mozzarella shredded
  • 1egg
  • * 1tbs green onion diced
  • 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Bacon pre-cooked
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato sliced
  • 1tbs mayo

Instructions

  1. Preheat the mini waffle maker . Ina small bowl, whip the egg. . Add the cheese, seasonings, and onion. Mix it until it's well incorporated. . Place half the batter in the mini waffle maker and cook it for 4 minutes. aR WON . If you want a crunchy bread, add a tsp of shredded cheese to the mini waffle iron for 30 secor The extra cheese on the outside creates the best crust! . After the first chaffle is complete, add the remaining batter to the mini waffle maker and cook Add the mayo, bacon, lettuce, and tomato to your sandwich.
  2. Enjoy! NOTES OMG! It was absolutely delicious!
