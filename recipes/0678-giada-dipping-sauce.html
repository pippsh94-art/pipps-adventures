Giada Dipping Sauce

Original cookbook page 678

Ingredients

  • + 2 tablespoons sun-dried tomato créme, such as agnoni
  • + 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar, such as guerzoni
  • * 1/2 teaspoon Giadzy spicy season or lemon and oregano salt
  • + 1/4 cup freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • * 1/4 cup chopped black cerignola olives, such as Fratepietro
  • + 1/2 cup olive oil
  • + 4 caper berries, soaked, rinsed, dried and chopped
  • ONC EMU trol lee coker \)

Instructions

  1. On a plate combine the sun-dried tomato créme, balsamic vinegar, season salt, pa cheese, cerignola olives, and olive oil. Mix together with a fork. Sprinkle the chopp berries on top and serve with crusty bread to swipe through.
