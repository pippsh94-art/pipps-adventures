Mozzarella Meatballs

Original cookbook page 31

Ingredients

  • 1.25 - 1.5 Ib ground turkey or beef
  • 1 medium zucchini grated
  • 1egg
  • 1/4 cup grated asiago or parmesan cheese
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp dried minced onion
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 8 oz mozzarella cheese cut into 18 cubes
  • 8 oz no sugar added pasta sauce such as Classico Tomato & Basil

Instructions

  1. Mozzarella Meatballs
  2. Preheat oven to 400. Spray 18 holes of two regular sized muffin tins with cooking spray. Or line easier cleanup and then spray.
  3. Stir together meat, grated zucchini, egg, grated cheese, garlic, onion, and salt. Divide the meat the prepared muffin tins. Press one cube of mozzarella into each meatloaf. Spoon a little sauce
