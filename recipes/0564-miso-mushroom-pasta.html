Miso Mushroom Pasta

Original cookbook page 564

Ingredients

  • Mushroom Medley
  • White Miso Paste
  • Heavy cream
  • Pappardelle pasta (or pasta of choice)
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Parsley for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of water and cook the pasta for 6-7 minutes, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
  2. In a large pan, add a tablespoon of butter and cook the mushroom medley for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Then add 1/2 cup heavy cream plus 1 tablespoon white miso paste.
  4. Add water if necessary.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and heat together for 1-2 minutes longer.
  6. Then plate, top with parmesan cheese and parsley and enjoy!
