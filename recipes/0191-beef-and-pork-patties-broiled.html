Beef and Pork Patties (Broiled)

Original cookbook page 191

Broiled beef and pork patties featuring bacon, onion, and garlic mixed into the meat for extra flavor.

Cook: 14-16 minutes • Total: 23 minutes

Ingredients

  • 4 slices bacon, diced
  • 1 cup minced onion
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1½ cups soft breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 tablespoon dried parsley
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • ¼ teaspoon marjoram
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground pork

Instructions

  1. Preheat broiler.
  2. In skillet, cook bacon, onion, and garlic until bacon is crisp.
  3. Drain; put into large bowl.
  4. Add all remaining ingredients; mix well.
  5. Shape into 8 patties.
  6. Broil, 6 inches from the heat, 7–8 minutes per side, or until done.
