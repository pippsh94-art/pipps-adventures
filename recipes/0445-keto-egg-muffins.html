Keto Egg Muffins

Original cookbook page 445

Ingredients

  • BASE:
  • 12 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped onion, (red, white or yellow/br
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • TOMATO SPINACH MOZZARELLA:
  • /4 cup fresh spinach, roughly chopped
  • 8 grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
  • /4 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • BACON CHEDDAR:
  • 1/4 cup cooked bacon, chopped
  • /4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • GARLIC MUSHROOM PEPPER:
  • /4 cup sliced brown mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup red bell pepper, (capsicum), diced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chopped parsley
  • /4 teaspoon garlic powder or 1/3 teaspoon minced garlic
  • NSTRUCTIONS
  • muffin tin with nonstick oil spray.
  • In a large bowl, whisk together eggs and onion.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F 180°C. Lightly spray a 12-cup capacity muffin tin with nonstick oil spray.
  2. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.
  3. Add egg mixture halfway up into each tin of a greased muffin tin.
  4. Divide the three topping combinations into 4 muffin cups each.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, until set.
