Cajun Mustard Creamed Cabbage

Original cookbook page 119

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Cajun Link Sausage
  • 1 small head green cabbage (or could use Hillshire Farm green onion, etc.)
  • 14-16 oz bag of Shredded Coleslaw (with or w/o carrots or tri-colored)
  • 1/2 oz Chicken Broth or Veggie Broth
  • 4 oz Cream Cheese
  • 1/2-2 Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 Tbsp Dijon Mustard
  • 1 tsp of Garlic
  • 1 tsp Parsley
  • 1 Tbsp Dried Minced Onions
  • 1/2 tsp Slap Ya Mama Hot(optional)...or regular pepper
