  4. Cutting the potatoes into roughly 1-inch pieces allows them to cook evenly and all the way through.
  5. The potatoes are placed on a trivet over water and cooked on high pressure for 4 minutes, but it will take your Instant Pot 15 to 20 minutes to reach the proper temperature/pressure for cooking.
  6. If anything they could firm up once refrigerated, which is why you need to cook them through
  7. completely, then let them cool before mixing with dressing.
