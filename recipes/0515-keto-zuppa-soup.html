Keto Zuppa Soup

Original cookbook page 515

Ingredients

  • Head of cauliflower
  • Spicy sausage
  • One onion diced
  • Garlic
  • Salt and pepper
  • Kale
  • Chicken broth
  • HWC
  • Fresh parm cheese

Instructions

  1. Brown the spicy sausage in a large pot, breaking it into pieces. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, saute the diced onion and garlic until softened.
  3. Cut the cauliflower into small florets and add to the pot.
  4. Add the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until cauliflower is tender.
  5. Add the kale and cook until wilted.
  6. Stir in the heavy whipping cream (HWC) and the cooked sausage.
  7. Season with salt and pepper. Top with fresh parmesan cheese.
