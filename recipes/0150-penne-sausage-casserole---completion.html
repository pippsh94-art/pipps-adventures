Final instructions and notes for the Penne & Sausage Casserole from page 149.

Cook: 20 minutes for cheese melting

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella cheese (as much as you like for topping)

Instructions

  1. Pour mixture into prepared pan. Top with as much mozzarella as you like!
  2. Place pan back into oven for 20 minutes or until cheese melts.
  3. Super good!
