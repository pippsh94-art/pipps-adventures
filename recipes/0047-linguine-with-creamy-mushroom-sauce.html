Linguine with Creamy Mushroom Sauce

Original cookbook page 47

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces whole-wheat linguine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, sliced

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook pasta according to package directions until al dente
  2. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat
  3. Add minced garlic and sliced mushrooms, sauté until mushrooms are golden, about 5 minutes
  4. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer
  5. Add cooked pasta and fresh herbs, toss to coat with sauce
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  7. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parmesan cheese
