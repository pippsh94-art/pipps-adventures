Corned Beef And Cabbage

Original cookbook page 775

Ingredients

  • 10 baby red potatoes, quartered
  • 4 large carrots, peeled and cut into matchstick pieces
  • Lonion, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 4.cups water
  • 1 (4 pound) corned beef brisket with spice packet
  • 6 ounces beer
  • % head cabbage, coarsely chopped

Instructions

  1. Step 2 Place potatoes, carrots, and onion into the bottom of a slow cooker; add water and place bris vegetables. Step3 Pour beer over brisket; sprinkle over spices from the packet and cover. Step 4 Cook on High for 7 hours; stir in the cabbage and cook for 1 more hour. Cook's Note Opt for a dark beer, such as a Guinness or another dry Irish stout, for the best flavor.
