Crispy Smashed Russets w/Green

Original cookbook page 36

Ingredients

  • 4 medium russet potatoes, scrubbed
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary
  • 3 tablespoons clarified butter, melted
  • 3 tablespoons grapeseed oil
  • 1 medium sweet onion, thickly sliced
  • 1 cup packed fresh parsley
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 teaspoons lemon zest
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground toasted
  • 246 / FAT13G (SAT 66) /

Instructions

  1. Combine potatoes, bay leaves and rosemary Bring to a simmer over high heat. Reduce heat Position a rack in lower third of oven; pre- Combine butter and oil in a small bowl. Pour brown, 15 to 20 minutes more. Meanwhile, to prepare sauce: Combine pulse until smooth. Serve the potatoes with the sauce.
