Keto Bacon Cheeseburger

Original cookbook page 12

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon —_ onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 pound bacon cooked and chopped
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 can tomato paste 6 ounces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 12 ounces cheddar cheese grated and divided

Instructions

  1. Cook bacon in oven. See above for link to easy method.
  2. Brown ground beef with garlic powder and onion powder. Drain (optional)
  3. In medium bowl, whisk together eggs, tomato paste, heavy cream, salt, and pepper until well combined. St into egg mixture. 4, Mix your sauce and cooked hamburger together and place mixture into casserole dish.
  4. Sprinkle remaining cheese and bacon over top.
  5. Bake at 350A°F for 30-35 minutes or until golden brown on top
