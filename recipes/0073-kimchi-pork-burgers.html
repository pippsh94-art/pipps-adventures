Kimchi Pork Burgers

Original cookbook page 73

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 1 cup sliced scallions, divided, plus 8 whole scallions, trimmed
  • 3/4 cup chopped kimchi, divided
  • 1 teaspoon fish sauce
  • 4 leaves napa cabbage
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 cup low-sodium broth
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 1 teaspoon gochujang
  • 4 whole-wheat toasted buns

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill.
  2. Mix pork, scallions, kimchi, and fish sauce. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Grill the patties to desired doneness.
  4. See recipe image for full details.
