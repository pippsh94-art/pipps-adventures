Golf Courses Within 20 Miles of Star, Idaho ⛳

A guide for the less-than-average golfer — rated for fun, forgiveness, and value

How to Read This Guide

  • Slope Rating: Average is 113. Lower = more forgiving. Higher = more punishing. If you’re a higher-handicap golfer, look for slopes under 120.
  • Course Rating: The expected score for a scratch golfer — lower means easier.
  • Bogey Rating: What matters most for you — but courses don’t always publish it, so slope is your best proxy.
  • Our Rating: ⭐ to ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — how much fun a less-than-average golfer will have here (factoring in difficulty, pace, price, and ego damage).

TOP PICKS — Quick Reference

Best for a Less-Than-Average Golfer (ranked)

  1. Ridgecrest Wee 9 — Lowest slope (90), short, zero stress (~$30)

  4. Warm Springs — Low slope (115), classic muni ($45–$55)

Best Value

  1. RedHawk — Under $30 for 18 holes!

Courses to Avoid Unless You’re Good

  • TimberStone — Slope 142, savage
  • Falcon Crest Freedom 9 — Slope 147!
