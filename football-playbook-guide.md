# Football Playbook: Zone Blocking & Bunch Passing

**System:** Zone blocking scheme with bunch passing concepts  
**Focus:** Inside/outside zone runs, tight formations, route combinations  
**Philosophy:** Simple assignments, multiple looks, explosive plays

---

## Zone Blocking Scheme

### Core Concepts

**Zone blocking** is a system where each lineman is responsible for a specific area (zone) rather than a specific defender. This creates consistent rules and allows for easy identification of run fits.

#### **Inside Zone (IZ)**
- **Concept:** Run between the tackles, follow double teams to linebackers
- **O-Line Rules:**
  - **Center:** Big-on-big (NT/MLB), work to backside LB if no threat
  - **Guards:** Big-on-big with Center/Tackle, climb to 2nd level
  - **Tackles:** Big-on-big with Guards, vertical set vs. pass rush look
- **Back Rules:** Press hole, work through B gap, cut based on flow
- **Key Coaching Points:** Stay square, maintain gaps, finish blocks

#### **Outside Zone (OZ)**
- **Concept:** Stretch defense horizontally, cut back against flow
- **O-Line Rules:**
  - **All linemen:** Reach block, get helmet to outside shoulder
  - **Backside:** Cut-off any penetration, work to 2nd level
- **Back Rules:** Get wide, press the edge, cut when defense overruns
- **Key Coaching Points:** Patience, vision, don't outrun your blocks

#### **Gap Scheme (Counter/Power)**
- **Concept:** Double team + pull for specific gap control
- **Counter Trey:** Pull guard and tackle, kick out + wrap
- **Power O:** Pull guard, fullback lead through hole

## Bunch Passing Concepts

### Formation Basis: **Bunch/Trips**

```
WR1    WR2    WR3
 X      Z      H
 |______|______|  <-- 2-3 yard spacing
         |
        QB
```

#### **Core Route Combinations**

### **1. Stick Concept (vs. Zone)**
- **Outside WR (X):** 6-yard hitch
- **Middle WR (Z):** 8-yard stick route (sit in zone)
- **Inside WR (H):** 12-yard dig/crosser
- **Read:** High-low the linebackers, Z sits in soft spot

### **2. Smash Concept (vs. Man)**
- **Outside WR (X):** Speed out (5-6 yards)
- **Middle WR (Z):** Corner route
- **Inside WR (H):** Seam/corner option
- **Read:** Pick/rub action, leverage defender with high-low

### **3. Flood/Mills Concept**
- **Outside WR (X):** 18-yard comeback
- **Middle WR (Z):** 12-yard square-in
- **Inside WR (H):** 8-yard drag
- **Read:** Overload zone coverage, work inside-out

### **4. Four Verticals (Air Raid)**
- **All Receivers:** Vertical stems, convert based on coverage
- **vs. Cover 2:** Bend routes to seams at 18-20 yards
- **vs. Cover 3:** Outside WRs convert to comebacks at 12-15
- **vs. Man:** Push vertical, use speed

**Route Definitions:**
- **0: Quick Game (Slants, Hitches)**
- **1: Quick Out (3-5 yards)**
- **2: Shallow Dig/In (6-8 yards)**
- **3: Deep Out (12-15 yards)**
- **4: Shallow Comeback (6-8 yards)**
- **5: Speed In/Slant (3-5 yards)**
- **6: Deep Comeback (12-18 yards)**
- **7: Corner Route (14-18 yards)**
- **8: Post Route (12-20 yards)**
- **9: Go/Fade Route (20+ yards)**

### **Quick Game Package (0-5 yards)**
- **Slants:** 3-step drop, quick release
- **Hitches:** 5-6 yards, sit vs. zone
- **Speed Outs:** 5 yards, get width quickly
- **Bubble/Now Screen:** Get ball in space immediately

### **Intermediate Package (8-15 yards)**
- **Digs/Crossers:** Work across formation
- **Outs:** Precise break at depth
- **Comebacks:** Stem vertical, break back to QB
- **Stick Routes:** Find soft spots in zone

### **Deep Package (18+ yards)**
- **Gos/Fades:** Vertical routes, back shoulder option
- **Posts:** Inside breaking, split safeties
- **Corners:** Outside breaking, beat deep coverage
- **Seams:** Up the hash marks

## Coaching Points

### **Zone Blocking Keys**
1. **Alignment:** Tight splits (6 inches), consistent stance
2. **Steps:** Six-inch lateral step, maintain base
3. **Hands:** Inside hand to breastplate, outside hand to armpit
4. **Finish:** Drive through hips, stay on blocks until whistle

### **Bunch Passing Keys**
1. **Spacing:** 2-3 yards between receivers in bunch
2. **Releases:** Clean off line, avoid picks by defense
3. **Timing:** QB and receivers must be in sync
4. **Reads:** Work high to low, inside to outside progressions

### **Quarterback Development**
1. **Footwork:** Consistent drops, balance in pocket
2. **Reads:** Pre-snap recognition, post-snap progression
3. **Decision Making:** When to check down vs. extend play
4. **Leadership:** Command huddle, make line calls

## Advanced Concepts

### **RPO Integration**
- **Zone + Slant:** Read inside linebacker
- **Zone + Bubble:** Count the box, throw if outnumbered
- **Zone + Stick:** Read middle linebacker leverage

### **Motion & Shifts**
- **Orbit Motion:** Create speed mismatches
- **Trips Formation Shifts:** Bunch to trips to create confusion
- **Tight End Motion:** Create different personnel looks

### **Game Situation Mastery**
- **Short Yardage:** Power/counter schemes
- **Red Zone:** Bunch formations for picks/rubs
- **2-Minute:** Quick game with checkdowns
- **Long Yardage:** Four verticals and deep shots
